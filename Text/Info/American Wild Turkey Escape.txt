American Wild Turkey Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit a american forest for your thanksgiving holiday. But unfortunately you noticed that someone trapped wild turkies in the forest. No one is there to help them. You have to rescue those wild turkies from this american forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
