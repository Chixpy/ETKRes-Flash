Special NEW YEAR issue of HALC SLOT game with SEXY SANTAS!
HAPPY NEW YEAR GUYS!

You can drag slot console anywhere with mouse.
Game controls: Mouse and keyboard
Enter - hide/show console.
SPACE - start reels.
Cursor UP - Raise bet
Cursor LEFT/RIGH - flip pages.
1-9 - starts slide show with 1-9 seconds delay.
0 or Cursor LEFT/RIGH - to stop slideshow.
ESC - exit to MAIN MENU
