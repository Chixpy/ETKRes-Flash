Lux Room Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game syou are working in a luxury house. You are asked to work for late night. And after finishing your work you came to know the door of the luxury room is locked from outside. No one is there to you help you out. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Try to find the key of the door. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles.
