Redwood Forest Turkey Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit an redwood forest for some research work. But unfortunately you came to know that someone trapped a turkey in this redwood forest. No one is there to help you. You have to help the Turkey to escape from this redwood forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
