Cute Girl Play School Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, without noticing a cute girl in rest room someone locked the play school. Unfortunately the cute girl is trapped inside this play school. No one is there to help her. You have to help the cute girl to escape from the play school by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
