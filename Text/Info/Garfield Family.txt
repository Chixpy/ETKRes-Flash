Garfield Family is a typical cartoon puzzle game. At this game you can choose various levels to play according to your expertise. After selecting the difficulty level, you will move on to the gaming screen where you will find shuffled and shattered pieces of Garfield and his family. Try to solve the puzzle before time limit. If you need more time you can remove the time meter. Play and enjoy at this cartoon puzzle game.

Instructions
Use your mouse to play this game.
