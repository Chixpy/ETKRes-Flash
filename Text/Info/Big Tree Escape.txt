Big Tree Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. This is the story of humans who are devastating the trees from the city as well as from forest for making buildings and for all type of constructions. After devastating the entire trees from the forest there is only one old big tree left. They are planning to cut the last big tree finally. Trees are the best gift given by nature to us which is controlling the pollution, so you want to save that tree and help to escape from there. Save that big tree to escape from there. For that you have to move that tree from the forest and help to place it in a hidden cave. Good Luck Have Fun!
