Tinkerbell Fairy World Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you came to a fantasy world to see Tinkerbell Fairy. But unfortunately someone trapped Tinkerbell Fairy and locked inside a cage. No one is there to help her. You have to help Tinkerbell Fairy to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
