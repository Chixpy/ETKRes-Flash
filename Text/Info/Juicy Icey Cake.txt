Get ready to build your own dream shop once again! You are running a kids shop in the city, where you have ice cream, cake and juice in your shop. Lots of customers started coming to your shop. Now you have to serve the customers. Give the customers their desired items, do not make them wait for long time, waiting time will be indicated, serve them before that or they will leave the shop. The number of juice items will increase in the subsequent levels, and limit will also increase. Good Luck!
