Easy Escape - Wooden House is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. Just Imagine, you are working in a wooden house, after finishing your work you started to your home. But the door of the house is not opening. You are locked inside the house. It's too late and no one is there to help you. You have to escape from there to using hints and some useful objects. Solve the puzzles for opening the door and escape from there. Good Luck!
