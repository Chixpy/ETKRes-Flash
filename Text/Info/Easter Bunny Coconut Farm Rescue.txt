Easter Bunny Coconut Farm Rescue is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to celebrate the Easter holidays in your coconut farm which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately, you noticed that someone trapped a bunny there. You have to find the way to rescue the bunny from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
