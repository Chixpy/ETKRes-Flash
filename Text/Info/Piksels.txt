Piksels, a Bart Bonte game.


Controls: cursor keys (+ r-key to reset a level). There are 16 levels. Enjoy!


'Piksels' was created for the lowrezjam (http://jams.gamejolt.io/lowrezjam2014), a game jam with the restriction to create a game with a maximum screen resolution of 32x32 (or upscaled)
