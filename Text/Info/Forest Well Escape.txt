Forest Well Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game a baby deer was playing in a forest. But unfortunately she fell down inside a well. There is no one to help her out. You have help the baby deer to escape from that forest well by finding useful objects, hints and by solving puzzle. Try to find the way to escape. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
