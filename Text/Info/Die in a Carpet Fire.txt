Ninja rugs is in despair, the Ghostly Gharpets company is making more profit than they do! To save ninja rugs, Ninja janitor must die in a carpet fire over and over again. By doing this, he will destroy the ghosts that possess the carpets. But it is not as easy as it sounds! This hardcore 2d platform game features: 40 levels, 4 stunning worlds, a super awesome boss fight, terrifying ghosts, an awesome storyline, the best music ever made, and of course a ninja janitor!
