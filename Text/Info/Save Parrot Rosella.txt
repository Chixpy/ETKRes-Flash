Save The Parrot Rosella is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, a young couple came to a beach resort with their parrot Rosella. But unfortunately, they missed the parrot Rosella. You have to find the way to save the parrot Rosella by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
