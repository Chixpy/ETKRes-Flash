Funny Bunny Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to see the beauty of a secret funny bunny forest which is located too far from your place. But unfortunately, you missed the way out. You have to find the way to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
