Haunting Halloween Pumpkin Escape is another new point and click escape game from wowescape.com. Halloween festival started. In this game you came to visit a pumpkin village for this halloween which is located at outer of the city. But unfortunately you missed the way out. No one is there to help you out. The place look very scary. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
