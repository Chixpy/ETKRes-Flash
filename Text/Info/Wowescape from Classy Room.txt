Wowescape from Classy Room is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. In this game you are trapped in a classy room room. The door of this classy room is locked and no one there because its already late. What will you do? Can you search the key directly or you have to need help of some clue and objects. Think! Show you escaping skill and search the clues and objects for escape from there. Good Luck.
