Abandoned RED 2 is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. this game is part 2 of Abandoned RED. This is a critical game where you are trapped in a dangerous abandoned house. This time again you are trapped inside another abandoned house. Do you think is it again possible to escape from a different abandoned house? If you have the right attitude then you can do it again. Lets show your courage and escape from this haunted abandoned house by collecting object, using clues and solve all the puzzles. Good luck and have fun!
