Today I went to take the golden ball inside the lunchbox. For that I want to break many bricks without leaving the ball on floor in each level. In this game your task is to break all the bricks and collect the golden ball from treasure box. You need to hit the bricks using the iron ball. Be careful you do not let the ball on the floor.  . Hit all bricks increase your score on the score board and Collect the golden ball to get more score. You have 3 lives only. Play and finish all the 25 levels and win the game.
