Dippin' Dots is the world's coldest Ice Cream and certainly the most unique frozen dessert. Imagine ice cream frozen into flavorful dots. Get ready to build your own dream shop once again! You are running a Dippin' Dots ice cream shop in the city, where you have to freeze the ice cream into colorful dots. Lots of customers started coming to your shop. Now you have to serve the customers with their flavorful dots. Give the customers their desired color, do not make them wait for long time, waiting time will be indicated, serve them before that or they will leave the shop. The number of color will increase in the subsequent levels, and limit will also increase. Good Luck!
