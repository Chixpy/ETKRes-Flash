Capercaillie Bird Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit a waterfalls which is located outer of the city. But you noticed that some trapped a Capercaillie Bird in that place. No one is there to help him. You have to help the Capercaillie Bird to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
