Escape from The Wolfman is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to see the beauty of a forest. But unfortunately, you noticed that two wolfmans trapped a boy in a cage. You have to find the way to save the boy from that wolfmans by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
