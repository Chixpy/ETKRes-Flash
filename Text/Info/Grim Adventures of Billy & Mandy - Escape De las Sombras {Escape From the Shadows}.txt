Game Instructions:

1) Use the "pink cloud" icon for Billy to climb the wall
2) Use the "green balloon" icon for Billy to land safely
3) Use the "pylon" icon for Billy to stop the other Billies (turns him into an obstacle)
4) Use the "bomb" icon to transform Billy into a time bomb (about 5-second timer)
5) Use the "chili" icon for Billy to drill through the ground
6) Use the "bridge" icon for Billy to build a bridge to cross gaps (5 pieces of snot are used to make the bridge)

Click the icon, then click Billy for him to perform the action.
