Drag and drop letters as you would in scrabble to form words, form more words faster to go farther.

Research and upgrade and see how far you can get, be warned you might learn something while playing this game!


1. The word fission reactor unleash the incredible force that binds letters as a word

2. Using the onboard computer, words have to be reassembled to provoke and energy surge

3. The energy is stored, and more words are supplied.
