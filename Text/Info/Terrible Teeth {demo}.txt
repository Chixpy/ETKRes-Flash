Play as Cornelius, a heroic kernel of corn trying to save his buddies from certain doom! You have been trapped inside the mouth of a radioactive monster with no escape! Run, jump, dodge and duck to avoid peril. Be warned: The mouth isn't the only danger! Watch out for tooth fairies and other obstacles along the way. This is a demo of the game available for free on Android and iOS. The demo is packed with over 30 mins of game play, but for a better experience, download the full version on for iOS or Android!
