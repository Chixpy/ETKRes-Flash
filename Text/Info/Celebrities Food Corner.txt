You have just opened your own fast food corner that too only for the Hollywood celebrities. Now it's time for the real work. The celebrities queue up to buy their favorite food. Prepare food and give the celebrities their desired food, do not make them wait for long time, waiting time will be indicated, serve them before that or they will leave the shop without paying the money. Serve as many celebrities you can with the given time to get target money loader and move to next level. The number of food will increase in the subsequent levels, and limit will also increase. Have fun playing this great kitchen game with all your favorite celebrities.
