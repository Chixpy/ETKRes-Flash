Baby Mammoth Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit a ancient forest. You heared a sound from a well. You noticed that a baby mammoth is fallen inside it. No one is there to help the baby mammoth. You have to rescue the baby mammoth from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
