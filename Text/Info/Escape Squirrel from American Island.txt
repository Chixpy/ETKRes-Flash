Escape Squirrel from American Island is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game a squirrel was trapped in a american island. She want to go back to her home. She feeling afraid and want to escape from that place. But there is no one to help you. You have to help the squirrel to escape from that american island by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Good Luck Have Fun!
