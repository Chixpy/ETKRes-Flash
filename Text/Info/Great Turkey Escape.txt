Great Turkey Escape is a type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. Do you like Turkeys? Tomorrow is thanksgiving day and someone trapped a Great Turkey in an unknown room for their thanksgiving day dinner. She is crying and there is no one near to help her out. Use your sharp mind to escape the turkey from that place. And give a chance her for giving thanks to you in thanksgiving day. Can you do it?Good Luck and Have Fun !
