Alligator Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit an Alligator forest. But unfortunately you missed the way of the Alligator forest. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this Alligator forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
