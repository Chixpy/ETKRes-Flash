Beauty Queen Castle Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to know that a Beauty Queen is trapped inisde a castle. But unfortunately, when you entered the castle to save her you noticed that the door of the castle is locked from outside. You have to find the way to rescue the Beauty Queen and to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles.
