Escape Game Save The Tourist Girl is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to see a forest which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately, you noticed that someone trapped a Tourist Girl in a cage. No one is there to help her. You have to find the way to rescue the Tourist Girl by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
