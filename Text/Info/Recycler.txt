Catch and drop the recyclable materials into the appropriate recycle bins. Use mouse to play. To catch and drop the recyclable material, click on recyclable material and release the mouse click to drop the recyclable material into the recycle bin. If you try to drop the recyclable material in unsuitable recycle bin, score will get reduced. Attaining target in each level will enable you to play further levels. To mute the sound, press p on the keyboard.  To bring the game to pause, press spacebar on the keyboard.
