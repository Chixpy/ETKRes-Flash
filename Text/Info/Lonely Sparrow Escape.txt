Lonely Sparrow Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit a greenish forest. But in this forest you noticed that someone trapped an little sparrow inside a cage. No one is there to help her out. You have to help the little sparrow to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
