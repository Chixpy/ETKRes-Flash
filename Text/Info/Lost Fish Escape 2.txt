Lost Fish Escape 2 is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. This is a new escape game series called as Lost Fish Escape. In this series a fish lover is having a gold fish in his house at aquarium. That person loves that gold fish very much. He is always keeping that fish with himself. He will go for boating every holidays. One day he went for boating with his loving gold fish. Unfortunately the fish jumped in to the deep sea. Now that person is feeling like he loses his lovely gold fish. The Gold fish also want go back to her owner. But he doesn't know how she will go back to her owner boat. The gold fish needs your help to go back to her owner. So in this series your mission is to get back the gold fish in to her owner. He is coming every holiday for boating and he is also searching his loving gold fish. Then find some clue, objects and solve puzzles to get back the gold fish to her owner. In this part of Lost Fish Escape 2 your target is you have to find the food for fish which will help you to escape that gold fish from the deep sea.After finding this take a rest and wait till our next part of lost fish escape. Good Luck Have Fun!
