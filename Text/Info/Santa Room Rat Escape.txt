Santa Room Rat Escape is a new point and click escape game developed by games2rule.com.In this Christmas games2rule brought different and very interesting escape games for fans. This games interesting story is based on a rat. That is trapped inside Santa room. As you know that in Santa Claus room have so many gifts in every Christmas for kids. So if rat will be there then he will destroy the entire gifts. Rat is also very upset there in that room. He is fear if someone catch him then they will kill the rat. You have to escape the rat from that Santa Claus room by using their available objects and gifts. Just Search the correct needed object and interact with puzzles and escape the rat from Santa room.
