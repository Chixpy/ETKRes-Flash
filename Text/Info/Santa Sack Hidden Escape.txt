Santa Sack Hidden Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to a forest to celebrate Christmas which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately, you came to know that someone stolen gift sack from Santa and hidden in the forest. You have to help Santa to find out the sack by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
