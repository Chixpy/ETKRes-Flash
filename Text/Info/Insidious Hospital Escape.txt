Insidious Hospital Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you came to see the Insidious Hospital which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately someone trapped you inside the Insidious Hospital. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
