Green Root Forest Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you came to see the beauty of Green Root Forest . But unfortunately someone trapped a squirrel inside this Green Root Forest. No one is there to help that squirrel. You have to help the squirrel to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
