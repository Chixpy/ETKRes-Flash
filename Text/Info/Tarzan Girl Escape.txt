Tarzan Girl Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, the Tarzan girl came to visit an ancient place deep inside the forest. But unfortunately she was trapped by some wild animals in that place. No one is there to help her. You have to help her to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
