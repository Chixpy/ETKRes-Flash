Yellow Mermaid Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game a yellow mermaid is living happily with her family under the sea. One day she caught between the rocks and she cannot able to come out of that. Now you have to help yellow mermaid to come out from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
