You are mighty. You are purple. You are an orb. The eternal power is with you. You have 5 little white balls that you can shoot to destroy terrain and other obstacles. Find your way out of 15 missions in this action/puzzle/skill challenge. There are 5 shooting challenges to test your accuracy, plenty of power-ups, many situations that will test not only your skill but also your analytical sense, speed and patience. Many medals to collect as well, 3 difficulty settings and of course one Super Extra Ultra Mega Hyper Uber Giga Final Mission (special edition) Please enjoy!
