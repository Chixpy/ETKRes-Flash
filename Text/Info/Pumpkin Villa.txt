CREEPY PUMPKINS-PUMPKIN VILLA is a terrifying point and click type new escape game developed by ENA Game Studio. You are in pursuit of golden pumpkins to feed it to the pumpkin man, You have to collect golden pumpkin from the pumpkin villa with the help of a witch. This villa is full of horror to cross it and to collect pumpkin.Collect objects and solve interesting puzzles. Get ready to face evil, keep playing ENA games.
