Pyramid Tomb Escape is a new point and click escape game developed by games2rule.com. In this new escape game you went for research about the pyramids and tombs, in that place there are so may pyramids. While watching and researching all pyramids you are get lost on your way out. Now what you will do? No one there for your help. Use your escaping skill, search for useful items, objects and solve the puzzles to complete the adventure case! Click on the screen to find items and use them when necessary and escape from there. Good luck and have fun!
