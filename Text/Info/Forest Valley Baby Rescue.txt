Forest Valley Baby Rescue is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this escape game, you came to visit an flower forest which is located at the top of the valley. You noticed that someone missed their baby and they shouting from bottom of the valley. You have to rescue the baby and handover to his parents safely by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles.
