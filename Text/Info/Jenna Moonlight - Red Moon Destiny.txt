Moonlight Dating Sim II : Red Moon Destiny
You play as Jenna Moonlight again, this time stuck in a spa resort with 50 days to spend until the Red Moon Rises. During that time, befriend others, suck blood and choose your destiny. Will Jenna become the Dark Princess and be lured by the darkness that is the Blood Pond? Or will she choose to fight the blood call?
