Escape Games: The Clown is the 715th point and click online escape game from 123bee.com. The clown always humiliates his assistant while performing. On the day of the show, the annoyed assistant plans to take revenge of the clown, so, he locks him inside the house and leave the place. Now, the clown has to escape from the house to perform in the show. Help the clown to escape by searching the key.
