Deep Lake House Escape is another type of escape game developed by wowescape.com. In this game, there is a guest house in middle of deep lake. People will go there through boat to take rest. One day one small girl visited that deep lake house. After enjoying for few hours she is ready to return, but the boat she came was went far from that lake house and stuck in middle of lake. She need some fuel also to fill in the boat. Now small girl was badly trapped there in that lake house. She needs someone?s help to bring back the boat near lake houseget and also have to find fuel for the boat. Help the small girl, search some clues, objects and solve some puzzle to escape the girl from there. Good Luck Have Fun!
