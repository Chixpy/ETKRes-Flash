Smash the Ghost is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you came to visit a old house which is located at outer of the city. But unfortunately that house is occupied by ghost. No one is there to help you out. You have to escape from there by smashing the ghost and by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
