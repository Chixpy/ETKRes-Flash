Duels for the best Pilot in the galaxy of Star Wars continue...Now duels will take place as if in times of a young Anakin and of the terrible Darth Maul, as well as in times of Rebel Alliance's victory and overthrow of Palpatine. Choose the fastest and mightiest aircrafts of the galaxy for both the good and the bad guys from the dark side of the Force, travelling from one star system to another one!
