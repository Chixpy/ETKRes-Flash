Just like with Hansel and Gretel, a wicked witch is out to satisfy the gaping hole in her stomach by abducting fresh, young children. Help settle her rumbling stomach and be the bearer of great gifts to share with the other witches as you fly to a party in the woods. Upgrade your gear on your venture such as your broom and cauldron. Additionally, equip hooks to be able to carry more children to your merrymaking. Be sure to cook and eat as much meat as possible but be careful, some angry Daddies are out with a vengeance to hunt you down! Features of this game included: - Upgrades - Leader boards - Infinite game-play - Mouse based controls - Astounding graphics - Box2D physics
