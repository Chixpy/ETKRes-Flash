Queen Turkey Escape is a type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. Do you like Turkeys? Tomorrow is thanksgiving day and someone trapped the Queen Turkey in an unknown place for their thanksgiving day dinner. She is feeling sad and there is no one near to help her out. Use your sharp mind to escape the Queen turkey from that place. And give a chance her for giving thanks to you in thanksgiving day. Can you do it?Good Luck and Have Fun !
