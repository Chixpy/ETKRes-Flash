Halloween Jack O'Lantern Rescue is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to a cementry for Halloween party. But you noticed that someone trapped and locked Jack O'Lantern with chains. No one is there to help him. You have to rescue him from that place by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
