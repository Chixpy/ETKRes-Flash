Abandoned Rescue and Escape 1 is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. This is a game series where a fearsome terrorist group has kidnapped our state president in an abandoned place. Your mission is to rescue the president and escape from that fearsome kidnappers without using any weapon or any noise. Because in that place terrorists are using some sensors to detect weapon or noise. If they hear any sound then you will be loss your great president. So you have to rescue the president using your mind and the available objects. In this first part of abandoned rescue and escape game you have to search the door key of that abandoned house after that you will continue your mission in our next part of this game. So start the rescue of our great president by using your mind and search some clues and objects for opening the door of the house. Good luck play Abandoned Rescue and Escape 1 Have a fun.
