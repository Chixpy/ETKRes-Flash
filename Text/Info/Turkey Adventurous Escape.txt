Turkey Adventurous Escape is a type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. Do you like Turkeys? Tomorrow is thanksgiving day and some tribes trapped a turkey in an unknown forest for their thanksgiving day dinner. She is crying and there is no one near to help her out. Use your sharp mind to escape the turkey from that forest and bring her back to the city. And give a chance her for giving thanks to you in thanksgiving day. Can you do it?Good Luck and Have Fun!
