Princess Bday Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Wowescape.com. Today is princess birth day and you are the close friend of princess so you are decorating the birth day party hall. While decorating someone by mistakenly lock the hall from outside and went. Now you have to do more preparation for birth day and you have to purchase gift also but how will you go out the door is locked from outside. Think deeply!!! Can you escape from there? Use your mind and search for some useful objects which will be help you to escape from there, solve the puzzles and escape from there. Good Luck. Have a fun.
