Andaman Tribe Island Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit Andaman Tribe Island which is located outer of Andaman. But unfortunately you missed the way out in that Andaman Tribe Island. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
