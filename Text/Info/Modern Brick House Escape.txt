Modern Brick House Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to a stay in Modern Brick House for your holidays. Unfortunately you are locked inside this Modern Brick House. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this Modern Brick House by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
