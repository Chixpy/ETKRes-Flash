1947th-GOLD MINE is the story of THE LOCKER new detective game developed by ENA GAME STUDIO. There was four scientists were killed and one was missed. We are in a job of finding the fifth scientist. We found a tracking device from workers house, which is most useful for this case. Now we have to find the fifth scientist in gold mine. It is not much easier unless you guess the clues.
