Grand House-Mini escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. In this game you are trapped inside a Grand House. In that house there is full of grand things. No one is near to help you out. You have to escape from that grand house room by finding items and by solving the puzzles. Collect related things and use them at correct way to escape from this room. Use your best escape skills to escape from there. Good luck and have fun!
