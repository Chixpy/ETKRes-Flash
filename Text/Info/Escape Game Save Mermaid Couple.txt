Escape Game Save The Mermaid Couple is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came under the sea to do some research. But unfortunately, you are noticed that someone trapped the Mermaid Couple in a cage. You have to find the way to rescue them by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle.
