Dungeon Adventure Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit an old dungeon which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately you missed the way out in that old dungeon. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
