Amazon Orchid Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to amazon forest to find the magical orchid flowers. But unfortunately you are trapped inside this amazon forest . No one is there to help you. You have to find the orchid flowers and escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles.
