Patio Escape is a point and click escape game developed by 8BGames.  Imagine that you were on a meeting in your Patio.  After meeting everyone went out by locking the Patio.  Unfortunately you were still inside.As everyone has gone out after the meeting.You cannot seek any help from others.You must find a way to escape from the Patio.  Find some hidden object to solve some interesting clues to escape the Patio. Good luck...Have fun!
