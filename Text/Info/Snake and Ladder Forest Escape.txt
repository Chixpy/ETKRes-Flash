Snake and Ladder Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this fantasy escape game, you came to visit an Snake and Ladder forest. But unfortunately you missed the way of this Snake and Ladder forest. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this Snake and Ladder forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
