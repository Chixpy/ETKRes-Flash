Aliens are attacking Earth... oh, wait; YOU'RE the aliens! UFOwned is a hectic and crazy pixel-shooter where your goal is to rack up as much damage and death as possible. Seek out specific targets to earn upgrades and either shoot them with your laser or beam them to your ship. Evade the ground troops and armored tanks and see how long you can survive. Unleash the Epic Beam for a crazy amount of damage!
