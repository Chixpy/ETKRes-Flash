You are the most Swaggy man in the world and you are the only one to save the electronic music.

Travel over the world and rescue all clubbing lovers.
Swag Man is a ""Pac-Man"" game genre in 3D ismometric view with the jump feature.
The game includes 5 different levels with 5 cool electronic musics.

Get the maximum of ""Music Notes"" and avoid Skulls. Jump over the skulls to collect ""Red Baloons"". Collect a lot of ""Music Notes"" to turn into the SUPER SWAG MAN, invicible and faster.
You have few minutes to reach the best score.
