Description: It's Halloween, and all your spooky and funny carved pumpkins are hidden under magical hats! Find all the pairs of matching pumpkins before time runs out, but beware the magical hats which keep swapping around! Score big bonuses by matching 3 in a row, and being fast.
Instructions: Click on the hats with the mouse to uncover the pumpkin underneath. Find the identical pairs of pumpkins.
