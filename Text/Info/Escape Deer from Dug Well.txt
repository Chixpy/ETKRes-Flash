Escape The Deer from Dug Well is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to see the beauty of a forest. But unfortunately, you noticed that a deer is fallen inside a dug well. You have to find the way to rescue the deer from the well by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
