Deadly Graveyard Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit the Deadly Graveyard. But unfortunately someone trapped you inside this Deadly Graveyard. The place looks very scary. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
