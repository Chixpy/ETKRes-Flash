Piggy Adventure Escape is a type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. In this game one girl living in between the forest. She is having some pets with her, but her favourite pet is a cute small piggy. One day unfortunately that piggy went far from the house and forget the way back. Now he has to do some adventure to come back at home. But piggy needs some ones help. Do you like to help the cute piggy. Just find some useful objects, clues and solve some puzzle for helping that piggy and help him to get back own house. Good Luck. Have Fun!
