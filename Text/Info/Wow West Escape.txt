Wow West Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Wowescape.com. You love the nature photography so you are looking for some good looking nature views and decided to go for photography in west. But you are gone misguided between desolate mountains. This place looks haunted. Now day going to end! You have to escape from this place before something miserably happened. Find a way to escape quickly! Good luck and have fun!
