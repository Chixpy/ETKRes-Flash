Fill up the darkest squares with the colored cubes. Color is Infectious, some squares will only be satisfied by certain colors. Activate buttons with boxes or crates to activate gates.
Push about crates with your boxes. Gravity Zones will push you about.

Use the mouse to create 100's of small colored boxes which you can then move about with the WASD or Arrow Keys.
