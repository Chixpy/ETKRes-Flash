Game Instructions:
Collect all the scrolls with your car before time runs out.
Hold the mouse key to switch directions, also watch out for the searchlights.

The four buttons on the title screen are: Easy Mode, Hard Mode, Challenge Mode(has an online leaderboard) and link to the leaderboards on their site.
