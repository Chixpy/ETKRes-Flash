Save The Halloween Pumpkin is another new point and click live escape game from WowEscape.com. In this game, you came deep inside a forest to celebrate Halloween holidays. But someone trapped few Pumpkins in this forest with their magical power. This place looks very scary. You have to find the way to save pumpkins by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
