Halloween Castle Pumpkin Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit a fantasy castle for your Halloween day. But unfortunately you noticed that someone trapped a pumpkin which is king of this castle. No one is there to help him. You have to rescue the pumpkin from this place by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
