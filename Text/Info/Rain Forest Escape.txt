Rain Forest Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com You are looking for some Adventure and decided to go for trekking alone in the nearby forest. But you are gone misguided to this isolated forest. This place looks haunted and has ferocious animals. Now day going to end. Scary noises are getting closer! You have to escape from this place before something miserably happened. Find a way to escape quickly! Good luck and have fun!
