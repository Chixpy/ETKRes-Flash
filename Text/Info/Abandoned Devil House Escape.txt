Abandoned Devil House Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this escape game, you came to an abandoned house to do some research. After a while you came to know that house is a devil's house. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this abandoned devil house by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
