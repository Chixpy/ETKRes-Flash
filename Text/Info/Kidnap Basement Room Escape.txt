Kidnap Basement Room Escape is type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. You was kidnapped and trapped inside in a Basement Room. The door of the basement room is locked. There is no one near to help you out. Outside of the room was surrounded by the kidnappers. You will be caught if you even make a small sound while trying to escape. Find some useful objects and hints to escape from the basement Room. Good Luck and Have Fun!
