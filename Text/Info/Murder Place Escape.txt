Murder Place Escape is a new point and click escape game developed by games2rule.com. In this Game one girl is doing part time housekeeping works in a house. One day she is come and going to clean the backyard of that house. While going she seen the house owner?s wife is killed by someone and her dead body placed in backyard. That Innocent girl is very scared and sited in a corner of backyard. Her mind is not working what?s going on. If police will come then they will arrest her because only she is there. You have to help that innocent girl. In that murder place there is seven evidences so you want to clear all for saving the girl. After clearing the evidence you have to open the back yard door which is lock from outside. Escape that innocent from there and rescue her. After Escaping the girl police will come and they will find the correct murderer.
