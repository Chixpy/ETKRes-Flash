Wow girls guest house escape is a another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. During this holiday season this cute girl out of city for celebrating the holidays and staying in a beautiful guest house. But after one night unfortunately she found herself locked in her room. There is no one near to help her. What will she do. She needs any intelligent one who rescues her from that guest house. Will you help this cute girl? Apply your intelligent thought and find some useful objects and hints. Solve the puzzles to escape the girl from the room. Good Luck Have Fun!
