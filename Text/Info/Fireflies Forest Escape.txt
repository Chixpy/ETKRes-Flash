Fireflies Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you you came to visit a fireflies forest for your research work which is located outer of the city. Unfortunately someone trapped the little princess of this fireflies forest. No one is there to help the little princess. Now you have to help the little princess to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
