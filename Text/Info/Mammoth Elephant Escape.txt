Mammoth Elephant Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game someone trapped a mammoth elephant with legs locked in a forest. No one is there to help the mammoth elephant. You have to help the mammoth elephant to escape from there by unlocking his legs. Escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
