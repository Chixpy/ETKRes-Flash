Pirates of the Caribbean Ship Escape is another new point and click escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, unfortunately you are trapped inside a Caribbean Ship. Pirates are sleeping now and they will awake in few minutes. Before that you have escape from that place. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
