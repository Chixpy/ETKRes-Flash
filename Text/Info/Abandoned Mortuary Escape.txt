Wowescape - Abandoned Mortuary Escape is another new point and click escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you came to visit a abandoned mortuary which is located at outer of the city. But unfortunately someone trapped you inside the abandoned mortuary. No one is there to help you out. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
