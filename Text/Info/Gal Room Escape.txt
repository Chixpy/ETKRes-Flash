Gal Room Escape is a new point and click escape game developed by Games2rule.com. One teen gal is celebrating holiday of Sunday at home. Surprisingly, her boyfriend phones her and asks her to come out. When gal tries to open the door, she realizes the door locked. Now she has been locked inside her room. Somehow she wants to meet her boyfriend. Gal needs your help now to escape from the room. Will you make her to escape from her room? Use your escaping mind and try to collect suitable objects and make use of it at appropriate places. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles and escape the teen gal from her room. Good luck and have fun!
