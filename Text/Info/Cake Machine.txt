Click on the first machine to pour the dough. The second machine is used to give shape to the cake. The different shape choices are displayed on top of the machine. The third machine gives the color coating to the cake. The colors available are also displayed. The fourth machine lays the icing. The next machine lays the topping on the cake. You have to click on the design once to get the desired topping. The last machine places the candle on the cake. You will have to click on the correct number to get a candle in that shape.
