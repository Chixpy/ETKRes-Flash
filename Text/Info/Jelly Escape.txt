Jelly Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game one dangerous green jelly trapped a female jelly inside a glass bottle. Her boyfriend jelly need someone's help now. You have to help the male jelly to escape the female jelly from the glass bottle. Help to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
