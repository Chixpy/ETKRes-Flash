Wet Clay Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to do some research in wet clay forest. But you noticed that a girl is trapped inside the wet clay. No one is there to help her. You have to find the way to rescue that girl from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
