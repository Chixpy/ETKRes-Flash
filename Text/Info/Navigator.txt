1495AD, 3 years have passed since a new continent was discovered in the West. Now the main European powers want to take control of those lands. As a representative of one of these countries, you must navigate to the right place, build a fortress and be the first to return. If you succeed, you will have claimed a whole New World for your nation. You can try to be the fastest sailing or destroy your rivals, any strategy is valid to achieve the goal.
