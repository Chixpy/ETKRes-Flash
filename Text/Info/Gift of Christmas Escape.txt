It is the 9th escape game from games2rule.com On Christmas Eve, your parents have bought a gift and planned to present it for you on Christmas day. As you are very eager to open the gift, you have decided to find out the gift while your parents sleep. As it is very offensive, never touch the unwanted objects found in the house as otherwise noise will get produced by objects and it will start to fill the noise loader. If the noise loader gets fulfilled, your parents will wake up and you will lose the game.
