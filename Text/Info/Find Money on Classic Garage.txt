Find Money on Classic Garage is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, A Grandpa passed away. He saved a lot of money in his Lifetime. He hides the money in one of his Car's Dicky. His Grandson needs that money to save the people who are affected by the flood. So now you need to help him to find the money. You have to find the money by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
