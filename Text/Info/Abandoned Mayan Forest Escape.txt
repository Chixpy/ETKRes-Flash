Abandoned Mayan Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit an abandoned mayan forest for some research work. But unfortunately you are trapped inside this abandoned mayan forest. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this abandoned mayan forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
