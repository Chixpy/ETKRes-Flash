Snow Pet Escape is a new point and click escape game developed by games2rule.com. This game is based on snow day tragedy in winter. In winter season everyone feels so cool and no one wants to go out from home. But in this escape game there is one pet which is trapped in a big snow region by someone. In that place the temperature is too low. The pet is totally frozen from snow falling. She needs to escape from that place. Otherwise she can?t be alive. She needs someone?s help to escape from there. Would you like to help that pet? Escape her from there and save her life. Search for clues and objects and solve some puzzles which will help you to escape the pet from there. Good Luck Have Fun!
