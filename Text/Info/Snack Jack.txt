Blackjack for pigs? No, it's Snack Jack!
Oink, oink, oink. You need to fatten up your pigs, farmer, and one way you can do that is by playing Snack Jack, a porcine variation of Blackjack.
Earn snacks for your pigs by making a total of 21 with the cards dealt to you and get enough snacks to reach the next level. Impress the judges and earn a ribbon for your prize porker.
