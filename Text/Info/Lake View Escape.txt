Lake view escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com. You are looking for some Adventure and decided to go for rowing alone in the lake nearby mountain. After reach the mountain you are viewing beautiful place. After taking rest you are ready to go back but unfortunately you are lose your oar anywhere. Now you are far from city so no one there for your help. You are alone between those big mountains. Day is going to end. You are hearing some Scary noises there! You want to escape from this place soon before end of day. Find your oar to escape quickly! But before that you have to search some clues, objects and solving some puzzles then you can find your oar. So start searching and escape soon from there. Good luck and have fun!
