Kill the Ghost is another new point and click escape game from wowescape.com. Halloween season started. In this game you came to visit a forest for this halloween which is located at outer of the city. But unfortunately you came to know that there is a bad ghost in this forest. No one is there to help you out. The place look very scary. You have to kill the ghost and escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
