Colorful House Escape is another new point and click escape game developed by games2rule.com. You are staying in a colorful house. Today you have so many works to do, so you are doing your work till night. After finishing your work, you felt hungry. So you decided to go out for a dinner. While trying to open the door you came to know that you are locked inside the house. It's too late and no one is there around the house. This colorful house is looking scary when you feel alone. You want to escape from there to using hints and some useful objects. Solve the puzzles for opening the doors and escape from there. Good Luck!
