Mini Escape-Girls Room is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. In this game you are trapped inside a girl room. In that room there is full of girls objects. No one is near to help you out. You have to escape from that girls room by finding items and by solving the puzzles. Collect girls reletaed things and use them at correct way to escape from this room.Use your best escape skills to escape from there. Good luck and have fun!
