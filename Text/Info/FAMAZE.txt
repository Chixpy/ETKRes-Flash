Which character will you choose for this exciting expedition? Will it be the knight, the thief or the wizard? This game is a blend of maze, puzzle and dungeon exploration adventure! The king is after the scrumptious rutabagas for supper. Before the king gobbles everyone up, you need to find the recipe to make a pudding out of the rutabagas in an attempt to save them! If he gets his hands on it first, he might just eat them all up.
