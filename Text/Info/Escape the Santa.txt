Escape the Santa is another point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.comAre you a great spy? This time Santa is in trouble. He has to go for Christmas gift delivery and he is trapped in a house which is behind of couple of forest. So help the Santa to escape from there. Before reaching that house you have to solve some puzzles in the forest. Show your intelligence and find the way of going that house and escape the Santa successfully. Have fun!
