Beach Lighthouse Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. One pleasent evening you came to beach and entered inside the lighthouse. But unfortunately someone trapped you inside in a beach lighthouse. The door of the beach lighthouse is locked from outside. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Try to find the key of the door. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun! .
