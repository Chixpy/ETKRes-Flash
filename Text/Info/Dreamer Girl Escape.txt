Dreamer Girl Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game someone trapped a girl inisde a house. The door of the house is locked from outside. No one is there to help that girl. You have help the girl to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Try to find the key of the door. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
