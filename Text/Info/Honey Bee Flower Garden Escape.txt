Honey Bee Flower Garden Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to to see the beauty of Flower Garden which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately, you noticed that someone trapped honey bees there. You have to find the way to rescue the bees from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
