Escape the Girl from Witch is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this fantasy game, you came to visit an imaginary fantasy world. You noticed that one bad witch trapped a girl in that fantasy world and made her as a statue. No one is there to help that girl. You have to rescue the girl by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
