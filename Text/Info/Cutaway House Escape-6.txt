Cutaway House Escape 6 is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com Another game from the series of cutaway escape games. You went for a trip outside and stayed in your guest house. After a snap you came to know that you are trapped inside in a cutaway house. The door of the house is locked. You want to escape from there by finding useful objects and hints. Find the right way to escape from this cutaway house. Good luck have fun!.
