Abandoned Forest Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. You are looking for some Adventure and decided to go for trekking alone in the nearby forest. But you are gone misguided to this isolated abandoned forest. This forest is looking too haunted and there are so many ferocious animals also. Now day going to end and still you are searching for way of escape. Scary noises are getting closer! You have to escape from this place before something miserably happened. Find the some clues and objects which will be help you to search a way to escape quickly from there! Good luck and have fun!
