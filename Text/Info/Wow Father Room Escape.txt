Wow Father Room Escape is another point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com This Christmas your father is angry with you. He trapped you in the house and locked the doors and gone to his office. You have to go to play with your friends because they are waiting for you. But how will you go? No one near at the house for helping you, have you any idea? You need to escape from there. Will you? Use your sharp minded to escape from the room. Good Luck and Have Fun!
