Little Girl Ghost House Rescue is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to know that a girl is trapped inside a ghost house. But unfortunately, when you entered the house to save her you noticed that the door of the house is locked from outside. You have to find the way to rescue the girl and to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
