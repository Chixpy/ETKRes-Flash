Halloween Creepy Cats Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit a scary forest for your Halloween party. But unfortunately you noticed that someone trapped four cats in a house. No one is there to help them. You have to resue that creepy cats by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
