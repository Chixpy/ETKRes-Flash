Slide the yummy candies to match 3 or more! Fill the big candy before time is up, to get to the next level. But beware the chewing gums that lock your candies! To get out of a sticky situation, use the small candy buttons to remove all candies of that type.
Instructions: Drag a row or column of candies with the mouse. Click a small candy button on the left, when it's filled, to remove all candies of that type.
