Spooky Jack O Lantern Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit a spooky forest for Halloween party. But unfortunately you are trapped inside in this forest. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this spooky forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles.Good Luck Have Fun!
