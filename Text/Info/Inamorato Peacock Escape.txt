Inamorato Peacock Escape is a new point and click escape game developed by games2rule.com. In this new escape game there are two peacocks. They are cute loving couple and both lived in a forest happily. One day unfortunately female peacock was trapped by tribal man in a forest house. Male peacock is worrying about his lover and thinking how she will escape from there. He needs your help to rescue his lover. You can rescue and escape that inamorato peacock by finding the objects and solve the puzzles. All the best!!
