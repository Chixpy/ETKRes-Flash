Tribe Girl Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit a forest which is located outer of the city to do some research. But you noticed that someone trapped a tribe girl in that forest. No one is there to help that tribe girl. You have to help that tribe girl to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
