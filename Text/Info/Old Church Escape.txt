Old Church Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game you came to see a old Church. You used to pray there when you was young. After spending few minutes you decided to move. But you came to know that someone trapped you inside that old Church. The door of the chruch is locked from outside. No one is there to help you out. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Try to find the key of the door. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
