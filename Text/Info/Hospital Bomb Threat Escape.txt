Hospital Bomb Threat Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to a hospital for a small treatment. After a small nap, you came to know there is a bomb threat in the hospital and everyone already left. You have to find the way to escape from that hospital by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
