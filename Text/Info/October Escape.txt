October escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com. In this game here is one baby who is in trouble because while cycling in his small three wheeler bicycle he goes far from his house in an unknown place. That place is so far from the baby house and forgets the way of his house. He starts crying and there is no one near to him to help. How he will go back safely his house. He needs someone?s help. Do you want to help him? Help the baby to find the correct way of his house, search some clues, objects and solve some puzzle and find the correct way of that baby house and make him happy. Good Luck Have fun!
