Mushroom Hut Escape is a point and click escape game developed by 8BGames. Imagine that you went to vacation to a fantasy forest. During the vacation you lost your Mushroom Hut key. You have kept all of your precious items in the hut. Find some hidden object and clues to solve some interesting clues to escape from the forest after collecting your things from the Mushroom Hut. Good luck...Have fun!!
