Banned Place Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. You are a reporter in a popular channel. You went to a banned place for examining a crime. After finishing your work you thought to return to office. But you noticed that you are trapped in that place. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Try to escape from that band place. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
