Jungle Tarzan Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you went to the jungle to learn about the Tarzan life style. But unfortunately someone trapped Tarzan in that jungle. No one is there to help him. You have to help Tarzan to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
