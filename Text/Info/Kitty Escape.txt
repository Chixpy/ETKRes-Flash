Kitty Escape is another type of escape game developed by wowescape.com. In this game one boy trapped a new one week born baby kitty. He loves that kitty so much but don't know how to show his love. So he always kept that kitty with himself. Even in night time that boy tied the kitty with the chain. The cute small kitty is crying and wants to go to her mother, but she can?t go. You want to help the kitty to rescue from that boy and escape from that house. Don't wake up the boy by making alarm sound. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve some interesting puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
