Rescue Alien Couple from Coniferous Forest is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you came to see the beauty of Coniferous Forest. After spending few hours there you came to know that someone trapped a alien couple in that forest. You have to help the alien couple to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
