Flower to Fairy Girl Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to a forest to do some research. But unfortunately, you came know to know that someone changed a fairy girl into a flower using their magical power. You have to find the way to rescue the fairy girl from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
