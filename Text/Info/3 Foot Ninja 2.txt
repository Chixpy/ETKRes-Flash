Originally published simultaneously on Miniclip and Newgrounds. May run too quickly on a modern CPU, causing accelerated gameplay.
Take an amazing journey in a far away place. Fight the evil enemies and return the Princess to the King of the southern lands. Use ARROW KEYS to move and A, S, D to fight. SPACE BAR crouch and block. Press 1, 2 or 3 to ACTIVATE respective BONUSES.
