Cactus Desert Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. You love the nature photography so you are looking for some good looking nature views and this time you decided to go for photography in cactus desert. But you are gone misguided between isolated cactus. This place looks haunted. All around you there is only cactus. Now day going to end. You have to escape from this place before something miserably happened. You have to search some clue and objects between that dangerous cactus. Use that objects and find the way to escape quickly from there ! Good luck and have fun!
