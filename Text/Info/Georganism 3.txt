We're not one to criticize anybody's weight, but if you find yourself being a gelatinous gob of goo, it might be time to get out of the house for a little exercise. That's just what you'll do as one of three unique jellies, sliding, swimming and sliming your way across a tropical island to collect flowers for its totems. Meld with other blobs to form new organisms and solve the island's puzzles. Are you ready for this jelly?
