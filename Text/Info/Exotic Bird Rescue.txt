Exotic Bird Rescue is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this escape game, you came to visit a lawn which is located outer of the city. You noticed that someone trapped a pair of macaw birds and locked in a cage. No one is there to help that exotic birds. You have to rescue the exotic birds by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
