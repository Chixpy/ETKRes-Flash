Blast your way through the autumn forest crushing enemies and stacking combos! Combos of GREAT MAGNITUDE! The dark creatures of the deep forest are gathering in hordes... Don't go down without a FIGHT! Samurai Autumn is a running action/platformer featuring 12 stages, 4 boss battles and upgrades! Stack combos by jumping on multiple enemies in succession. Power your blade with Sun Flowers and unleash a furious storm of energy blasts.
