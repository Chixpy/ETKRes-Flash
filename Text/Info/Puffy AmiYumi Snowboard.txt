Puffy AmiYumi Snowboard is one of the newest games that appeared in this category where you have to have skill and agility. In this game you will need these qualities because only you will accomplish your objective successfully. Our heroines from Cartoon Network have reached the North Pole where they will give snowboarding. You will need to use the arrow keys to move so keep your eye on mountain obstacles: rocks, animals, trees and so on. If you get into something you will lose a life and if you run out of all the lives you have to start the whole game from the beginning. The more you collect more points so you'll have less chance to enter the top of the best players. In front it is Ami but if you want to change, if you want to take the place Yumi must press the space key. Give of your best to reach the top of the best players, because only you show everyone what you are capable.
