Play this puzzle with lines by completing these geometric shapes. Click on the mouse and drag it from one yellow point to another in order to connect the lines. The purple point indicates the position from which you should continue the structure You score up according to the number of lines you connect You lose the game as the circuit gets closed, which means that you cannot continue further Complete the structure before the time runs out and move on to next levels Complete all the 15 levels successfully to win the game.
