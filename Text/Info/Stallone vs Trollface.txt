In this game you'll breach your way through walls using different explosive devices, To surprise enemies from different angles, as your bullets tend to ricochet from the walls, and rescue hostage women.
Game can be completed just by rescuing hostages, there's an achievement for doing the Pacifist route.
Controls: WASD = Move; Mouse = Aim and Shoot; Spacebar = Throw/Detonate bomb
Feel yourself real Sylvester Stallone and fight to free hostages being captured by evil trollface meme. Good luck on your noble mission!
