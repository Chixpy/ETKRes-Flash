Christmas Chimney Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, While getting ready Santa came to know that he was locked inside his house. Its already late he need to distribute gifts. He don't know how to escape from there. You have to help Santa to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
