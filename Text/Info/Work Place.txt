1958th-WORK PLACE is an normal room escape game created by ENA Game Studio. Bruter in a need of collecting missing objects from the museum. Bruter went to small IT company to find a pen which was more costly in the world. Because last week the man who came from the company took that pen and hide inside his cabin. Now Bruter went to that office as a cleaner to find the pen.Have greater excitement and fun playing escape games.
