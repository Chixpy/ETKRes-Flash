Lion Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com. In this new escape game one African lion miss guide his path and reached a city during mid night time. Unfortunately some zoo guards noticed him and they wants to catch him. You have to help that wild African forest lion to escape from there and return to his forest through metro train. For sending back him through metro train you have to need some objects like ticket and all so you have to gather all the necessary objects to rescue the lion. Use your escaping mind and search for that necessary objects by solving some puzzle and send back the lion in forest. Good Luck Have Fun!
