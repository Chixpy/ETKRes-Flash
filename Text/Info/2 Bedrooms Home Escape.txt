2 Bedrooms Home Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com. You thought to buy a 2 bedroom home and went to see it nearby your street. The land lord opened the door of that house and went for his another work. While watching the house suddenly the door is lock from outside and you are trapped there. This house is new for you so you are in big trouble you have to escape from there. . No one there for opening the door. Think how will you escape? You should search for some clues and useful objects which will be helpful for your escape from there. Good luck. Have Fun!
