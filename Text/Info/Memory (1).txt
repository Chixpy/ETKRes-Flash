Memory is a puzzle game on www.t45ol.com, which challenges you to find and match the components that make on line the work. Your objective is to flip cards to find matching pairs , which have the same patterns,such as peach, fan, bow, gourd,demijohn, bell and so on. All cards are random arranged. You must find two that match. Each pairs gives you 10 points,but match wrong pairs decreases you 5 points . Remember you have limited time. You should match them before the incense runs out,or the game will be over. There are many levels, which are given to you to choose. Go ahead and explore various exciting levels...
