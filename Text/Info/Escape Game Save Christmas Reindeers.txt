Escape Game Save The Christmas Reindeers is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to a forest to celebrate Christmas which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately, you came to know that someone trapped few Reindeers in that forest house. You have to help the Reindeers to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
