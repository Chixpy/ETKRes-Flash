Bigshot Room Escape is a new point and click escape game developed by games2rule.com. You are a bigshot of your city and your living place is too hi-fi and modern. Today you have to go for an important meeting in out of city. Now you are ready to go but when you are trying to open your rooms door it's locked and it is locked from outside. Now you are in trouble because in meeting there are lots of bigshot people are waiting for you and they can't wait too much. So you must have to escape immediate from your room. Always you are using your mind for business and now you are one of the success people in your city but today you have to use your mind for escape from your room and attending our important meeting. It's great challenge for you how will you escape from there. You know each and every thing about your room so search the clues and useful objects and use them for escape from there. Good Luck. Have a fun.
