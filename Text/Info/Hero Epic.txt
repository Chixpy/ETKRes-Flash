Fight the evil hordes over 20 levels and 5 different stages! Conquor the land! complete the stage by reclaiming all the strips of land with your troops! Use gold to buy in units. The more land you have the greater your gold increases! Collect money bags to increase your funds! Upgrade your units attack, defence or speed at the shop between levels! Use builders to put down walls (they automatically put them down at the last piece of ground and then expire) to halt your enemies progress!
