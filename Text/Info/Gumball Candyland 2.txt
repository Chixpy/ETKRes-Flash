Gumball Candyland 2 is a great platform game. Gumball and his friend Darwin are lost in Candyland. You need to help them to get out of there. You need to collect all of the candies and put them on the cake. You need to finish all 10 levels. To control Gumball use arrows keys, for control Darwin use W,A,D . Good luck !
