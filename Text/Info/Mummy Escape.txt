Mummy Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com. This is the story about saving our world from evil. They are going to occupy the world after killing all the people. But fortunately people have one option. There is a good mummy which is committal in a pyramid. You have to wake up that mummy from that pyramid to save the world from evils. Find some clue, objects and interact them with puzzle to escape the good mummy from pyramid. Good Luck Have Fun!
