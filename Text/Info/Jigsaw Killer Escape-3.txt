Jigsaw Killer Escape-3 is another new type of point and click escape game from games2rule. Last time, you have escaped from the trap set by Jigsaw killer. So, this time, Jigsaw killer set a new trap in which you will have to save a stranger, where the stranger is locked up with chest bomb. Defuse the bomb within 45 minutes as otherwise bomb will kill the person and the room door will get locked permanently. Get the key within the time duration and escape from the place. Good Luck and Have Fun!
