Halloween Haunted Castle Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit a haunted castle on Halloween day. But unfortunately you noticed that someone trapped some Halloween characters inside this haunted castle. No one is there to help them. You have to help them to escape from this haunted castle by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles.Good Luck Have Fun!
