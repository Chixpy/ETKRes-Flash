Little girl and the turkey is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In 1840, a little girl was pursued by a wild turkey. She ran and climbs up the tree. There is no one nearby to help the girl. Turkey was waiting under the tree for the girl. You want to help the girl to escape from there by finding useful object, and hints. Find the right way to escape the girl from the turkey. Have a fun game play.
