Pumpkin forest escape is a new type of point and click escape game created by games2rule.com In this escape game Halloween pumpkin unfortunately trapped in a pumpkin house and the door is locked. There is no one near to help the pumpkin. So help the Halloween pumpkin to escape from the pumpkin house by using some objects from there. You can escape step by step only. So don?t miss the objects to collect before you go to further step otherwise you have to fall again into the previous step and collect the objects than play the same steps again. Finally the rescued pumpkin will go to celebrate the 2013 happy Halloween festival. All the best!
