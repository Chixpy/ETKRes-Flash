Miraculous Daylight Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit a forest which is located outer of the city. In that daylight forest you came to know that someone trapped a peacock. No one is there to help her. You have to help the peacock to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
