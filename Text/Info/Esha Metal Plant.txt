1962nd -ESHA METAL PLANT is a new escape game developed by ENA Game Studio. Bruter reached the factory where the thief kept a stolen object from the museum. Bruter is searching for a missing object inside a factory. The factory contains full of old equipment, because it is very hard to find from that place. It is your responsibility to help Bruter to find it as soon as possible. Solve some interesting puzzles and have fun in playing escape Games.
