Shooter job-3 is the third stage of training in WPF (world protection force) .In this stage you have to take Gun-shoot training in Target 10m board. Assemble quickly the separated parts of 1911 pistol and load the bullets on the magazine to get maximum points. Shoot 10 ring moving Target board before reach its distance, each ring having different shooting points. You have to use 10 bullets magazine for each board. Complete the boards in minimum time to get more points .The time, distance, ring shoots, gun and magazine assemble points will calculated as your training results in each of the levels. Good luck for your shooter job.
