Toucan Couple Escape is a point and click escape game developed by 8BGames. Imagine that you went to a fantasy forest as an adventurous trip. While you were enjoying your adventurous trip lonely. You noticed a cute Toucan locked up in cage and its pair to trying to save its pair. Someone might have locked up for illegal trading. Find some hidden object to solve some interesting clues to escape the Toucan before illegal traders arrives. Good luck...Have fun!
