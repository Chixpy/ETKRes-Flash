Escape Game:Save the Caterpillar is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this escape game a big spider trapped a caterpillar in his web. But at this caterpillar is getting ready to change pupa. You have to help the caterpillar to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
