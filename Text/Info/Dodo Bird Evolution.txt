Dodo Bird Evolution is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. The Mauritian "calvaria"tree, soon after the dodo bird became extinct, stopped sprounting seeds, and it appeared it would soon face extinction itself. While it was not initially apparent, the calvaria would only sprout seeds after having been eaten and digested by the dodo bird. In this game, you have to find the way to survive dodo bird as well as doodo tree by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
