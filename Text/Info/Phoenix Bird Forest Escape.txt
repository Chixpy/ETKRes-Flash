Phoenix Bird Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. The Phoenix bird is often referred to as a fire bird, because it dies and is reborn out of fire. The ancient beliefs that phoenix tears can heal wounds. In this game, you came to a phoenix bird forest to save a wounded girl. You have to find the way to save the girl by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
