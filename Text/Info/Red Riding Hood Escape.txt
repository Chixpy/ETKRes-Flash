Red Riding Hood Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. Red Riding Hood is a European fairy tale about a young girl and a Big Bad Wolf. In this game we have tried to do some part of the story as a escape game. You have to help the Red Riding Hood to escape from the fox. Escape Red Riding Hood by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
