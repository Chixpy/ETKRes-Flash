Underwater Cave Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game you are a journalist. You came to a underwater cave for collecting some news about that place. After few minutes you thought to move but you lost the way to exit. No one is there to help you out. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
