1954th-RUN WAY is an new escape game created by ENA Game Studio. Presume a situation there was a robbery in a museum, 20 objects were stolen. But cops arrested Bruter,because he was a most wanted criminal in two years ago. Before few months he left all this robbery and become a good person in a city. He asked to police that he will find all the objects and show his royalty.First,Bruter went to airport to find a statue. Keep on collecting objects and solve some interesting puzzles.Good luck and best wishes from escape games.
