Original Description: Fashion Cat needs a stylist! Dress up Fashion Cat any way you like. Choose clothes and accessories from the left hand menu and click on the item you want to use. Once you've dressed up your cat, click 'done' to go through to see your finished outfit.  You can then print your cat out by clicking on the printer icon in the top left of the window.  Make sure you have a printer  set up on your computer. Have fun!
