Turkey asylum escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. Hello wowescape fans, in this festival of thanksgiving we brought a game series for you called turkey asylum escape. In this game series you have to save the turkey in a good asylum where no one can catch and kill the turkey. In this game you have to find 1 gold turkey coin also in every part which will be helping you in final part to finish the game series. It's too interesting. In this 1st part of this turkey asylum escape game you have to find the first gold turkey coin. So let's start to find asylum for turkey Good Luck. Have a fun.
