Tribe Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. One pleasent evening 2 bad tribals trapped a tribe girl and placed in their hut. His boy friend decided to rescue his girl from that 2 bad tribals. But he cannot do alone without your help. So you have to help that tribe boy to rescue his girl friend from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
