Handicap Escape is a very special and interesting escape game for escape game lover presented by wowescape.com. In this game one maniac boy in a city which is always harass the neighbour. One day he catches a handicap neighbour boy and takes away in a top of mountain. He placed that handicap boy at the core of the mountain and kept two stone in the wheel of his wheel chair. Now no one knows at the handicap boy house where is the boy. After leaving the handicap boy, maniac boy comes back at house. Now what he will do? He is struggling too much for going back to home. He needs someone?s help for going home back. You want to help that helpless boy to escape from there. Find some clues, objects and clear the for that boy. This adventure will come to an end after successfully when the boy reaches his home. Good Luck Have Fun!
