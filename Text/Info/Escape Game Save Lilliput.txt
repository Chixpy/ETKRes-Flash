Escape Game Save The Lilliput is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to see the beauty of a forest which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately, you noticed that someone trapped a lilliput in a cage. You have to find the way to save the lilliput from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
