Honey Bees Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit a Honey Bees Forest which is an fantasy world. But unfortunately you missed the way out. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from that place by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
