Mini Escape 3 is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. Hello games2rule fans. We again brought a new escape game of mini escape series. This is the 3rd part of mini escape called mini escape 3 . In this game you are again trapped inside a mini house. The door is closed by somebody from outside. So, you have to escape from that mini house. Find the main door key. You have to search for clues, Objects and solve some interesting and puzzles for finding the unlocking door key. Use your best escaping skills to escape from that house. Good luck and have fun!
