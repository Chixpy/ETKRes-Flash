Elsa Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. This is a brand new escape game for wowescape fans. The story of this game is related to the snow queen Elsa when she was a kid. One day while she was walking on Iceland and crossed a river though her magical power. But after crossing it she unfortunately lost her power. Now she is trapped alone in snowland. River is very deep so she can?t cross without magical power. To return back into the castle she needs her magical power back to make a path for crossing the river. The snow queen needs someone?s help to get back her magical power. You have to help the Elsa to get her power back and return to castle. Search some useful clues, object and extract them with puzzle to escape the Elsa from that place. Good Luck Have Fun!
