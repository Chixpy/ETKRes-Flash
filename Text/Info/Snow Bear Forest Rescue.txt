Snow Bear Forest Rescue is a type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com . In this game, you came to visit a snow forest which is located outer of the city. You noticed that someone trapped a snow bear in a cage. No one is there to help him. You have to rescue the snow bear by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
