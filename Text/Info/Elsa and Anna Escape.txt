Elsa Castle Escape is a type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. This escape game is based on a very famous movie Frozen. In this game Elsa and Anna is trapped different ice mountain castle. They are locked inside the castle and there is no one there to help them. They all need someone?s help to escape from there and back to their own world. Will you help them? Find some clue, objects in that ice mountain and use them for solving puzzle and rescue all them. Good luck Have Fun!
