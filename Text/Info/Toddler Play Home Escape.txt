Toddler Play Home Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. Hello games2rule fans. We again brought a new escape game. In this game you are trapped inside a toddler play house. The door is closed by somebody from outside. So, you have to escape from that toddler play house. Find the main door key. You have to search for clues, objects and solve some interesting puzzles for finding the door key. Use your best escaping skills to escape from that house. Good luck and have fun!
