Beach Lawn House Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you decided to buy a beach lawn house. So you came to visit a house which is located outer of the city. Unfortunately you are trapped inside this beach lawn house. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this burning house by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
