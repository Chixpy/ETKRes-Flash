Owl Forest Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game someone trapped a female owl in a dangerous forest cave. Male owl is feeling sad and no one is there to help him. You have to help the male owl to rescue the female owl from the dangerous forest cave. Escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
