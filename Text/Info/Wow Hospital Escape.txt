Wow Hospital Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. You came to see a doctor for a check up in a hospital. You went to rest room and decide to move home. But the door of the hospital is locked from outside. You noticed no is there in the hospital. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Try to find the key of the door. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
