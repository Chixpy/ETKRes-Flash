Rapunzel Ear Doctor is an online game that you can play on 4J.Com for free. Rapunzel feels awful for a few days because she has an infected ear. She might lose her hearing forever, but fortunately for her, the help has come. Play this game and use the tweezer to remove the microbes from her ear, use the syringe to remove the infected areas, the magnifier to see if there are tiny microbes
