Save the Black Panther is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to see the beauty of a forest which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately, you noticed that someone trapped a Black Panther in a cage. You have to find the way to rescue the Black Panther from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
