In this action-packed avoider you must use your skills to guide the brave little ant as far up the perilous bathroom wall as possible. Use your quick reflexes to dodge droplets and race from splashes. Utilise a variety of fun bathroom accessories to pave your way across countless tiles. Beware of wasps whose sting are worse than their buzz and slippy soapy globs. Grab a bite on the way up for health or catch a ride in floaty bubbles for a big oxygen boost.
