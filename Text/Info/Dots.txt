Dots, dots, dots, smile. What does this mean. Well, this is a game about a dot trying to connect to another dot. This might not be the usual pixelated game that you can find here at ClassicGames.Me, but ... :D challenges your mind. You must use your hand and brain in beating the 24 levels of greatness. This was originally made as an entry for Ludum Dare #26 but was remastered with new graphics, levels, and difficulty balanced! controls: Move [Arrow keys]
