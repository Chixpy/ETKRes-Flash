Christmas Tree Decor Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to a forest to celebrate Christmas which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately, you missed the way out. You came to know that Santa will come there if your decor the Christmas tree and he will help you to escape from there. You have to find the way to decor the Christmas tree by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
