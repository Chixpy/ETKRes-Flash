Santa Christmas Gifts Escape-1 is a type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com . This Christmas, Santa is going to deliver gifts for seven special kids from seven different places in the world. Totally there will be 5 parts in this story. In this game, he starts his journey and going to be delivering gifts for first kid but unfortunately Santa's sleigh is trapped in the storm and fell down in a snow morass. There are two reindeers first one is hungry and the second one is sleepy. Santa needs your help. Help him and use your mind, arrange food for first reindeer and wake up second reindeer to escape Santa from there and deliver the gift successfully to the kid. Enjoy first part and Have a fun!
