Genie World Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this fantasy game, you came to visit a Genie World. In this Genie World you came to know a little boy missed his parents and standing alone. No one is there to help that you. You have to help the boy to find his parents also the way to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
