Santa Christmas Gifts Escape-5 is a type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com This Christmas, Santa is going to deliver gifts for seven special kids from seven different places in the world. Totally there will be 5 parts in this story. In this part Santa is going for delivering 7th gift to the kid in Antarctica. But unfortunately Santa forget to bring his gift. Santa needs to go back to North Pole to take the gift back and travel again to Antarctica. Let's help Santa to delivering a 7th gift to the kid. Enjoy fifth and last part of this series. Happy Christmas to all and Have fun!
