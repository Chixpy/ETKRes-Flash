Cool Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit a cool forest which is located outer of the city. In this cool forest you came to know that a small boy is fallen inside a sink hole. No one is there to help him. You have to help the little boy to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
