Lovers Kiss 2015 is a romantic kissing fun game developed by games2rule.com. This is a valentine's day special game, where one couple are taking a break from dancing on their Valentine's Date to share a romantic moment together beside the club. But Valentine's Day isn't only for lovers! It's also for the old people and weirdness who just want to stop cute young people from kissing! Kiss your Valentine and don't let others catch you, and hurry, enjoy the 2015 valentine day. Have Fun!
