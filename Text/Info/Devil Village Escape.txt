Devil Village Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you came to see a ancient village. After spending few hours there you came to its a devil village. You tried to move from there but you missed the way. No one is there to help you out. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
