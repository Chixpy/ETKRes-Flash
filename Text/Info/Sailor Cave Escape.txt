Sailor Cave Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game while a ship travelling to a island it was break down. The sailor thought to stay in cave night and back to city in the morning. After sunrise he came to know that some one trapped him inside the cave. No one is there to help him out. You have to help the sailor to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
