A drinking Game! Throw the ping-pong ball into the any of the cup found on the table.Select the type of angle and power range from the panel.When the angle and power is selected, click on the ball to toss it.If you fail to throw the ping-pong ball into the cup, you will have to take a gulp of beer.If the beer level exceeds the bar, you will lose the game. Complete each level successfully to unlock the young girl dress, one by one.
