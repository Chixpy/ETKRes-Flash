Autumn Forest Elephant Rescue is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit a autumn forest. But unfortunately you noticed that a baby elephant is fallen inside a sand hole. No one is there to help her. You have to resue the baby elephant from sand hole by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
