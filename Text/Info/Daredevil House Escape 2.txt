Daredevil House Escape-2 is a type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. You went out for a trip and rented a house. While you went inside of house the door of the house is automatically locked and you are trapped inside. The house is full of devils. You want to escape from there by finding useful object, and hints. Find the right way to escape from the Dare Devil House and save your life. Good Luck Have Fun!
