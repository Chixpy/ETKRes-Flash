Forest Guest House Escape is a new point and click escape game developed by games2rule.com. The group of your friends come to the forest for your vacation holidays and stay in a guest house which is between the forests. In morning they forget you and get back leave you alone there. The guest house owner locks the guest house door and compound gate also. Now you are lonely trapped between the forests. This is a challenge for you. Now you have to find the door key of that guest house then you have to realise the two leopard dog which are the watcher of that guest house after you have to find gate key and escape from there. Collect the necessary objects use them for solving puzzles and escape from the forest guest house. Good luck and have fun!
