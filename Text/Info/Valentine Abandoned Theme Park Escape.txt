Valentine Abandoned Theme Park Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit an Valentine abandoned theme park to give a suprise to your girlfriend. But unfortunately you are trapped inside the Valentine abandoned theme park. No one is there to help you. You to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
