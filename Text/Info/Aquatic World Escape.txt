Aquatic World Escape is a new point and click escape game developed by games2rule.com. In this new escape game, you are traveling on a ship to a island for a vacation. Suddenly the ship collides with an iceberg and sea water started occupying the ship. After sometime you sink inside the deep sea with the ship. Inside the sea its look likes another world. Try to escape from there soon because you can?t survive long time inside the water. Find some clues, objects and click on the object to interact and solve puzzles for escape from there. Good Luck Have Fun!
