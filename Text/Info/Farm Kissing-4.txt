In this Farm kissing game Jenny wants to guard the piggy farm. Her boyfriend arrives to the farm. They get into romantic mood, so make them to kiss each other by clicking on them. While kissing you have to feed the piggy, catch and drop the piggy into the farm house when it tries to escape. Get the trapped piggy out from the farm house if they wants food otherwise it will die. Fill the kissing loader within specified time duration in each level. Keep in mind! If a piggy escape or dead you will lose a life. Feed the piggy before the food getting empty otherwise all piggy will escape from the farm house and the game will be comes to an end.
