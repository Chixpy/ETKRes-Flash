1968th-THE OFFICER'S HAT is an point and click type new escape game powered by ENA Game Studio. Presume a situation there was a robbery in a museum, 20 objects were stolen. Bruter in a pursuit of collecting missing objects from museum. Now Bruter went to the forest house to find a hat from that house. Now you have to help Bruter to find an object as soon as possible. Good luck and best wishes.
