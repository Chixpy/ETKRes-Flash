Pandora Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit Pandora Forest with help of a dragon. But unfortunately somone trapped your dragon and trying to trap you. No one is there to help you. You have to rescue your dragon and also escape from this Pandora Forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
