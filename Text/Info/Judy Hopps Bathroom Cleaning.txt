Judy Hopps Bathroom Cleaning is a new game appeared on our site in which you need skill and agility to finish the cleaning before the time runs out. If you are not fast enough in this game with Judy you have to start the whole game from the beginning. You need the mouse to complete your tasks, to make the bathroom shine, to do the cleaning as fast as you can in this game from Zootopia games category. Use the mouse to wash the floor, the toilet, the bath tube, to clean up the windows, to put all the things at their place, to pick up the garbage and so on in this game with our heroine from the movie, Judy Hopps. In this game you will get all the necessary information required to accomplish your tasks successfully, so you don`t have to worry you don`t know what to do or what buttons you need. If you finish quickly the cleaning you can enter in the top of the best players but for that you have to be hard working, to make all the tasks in a short time.
