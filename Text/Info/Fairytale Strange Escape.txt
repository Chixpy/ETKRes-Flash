Fairytale Strange Escape is type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. While Fairytale was in a search of treasure, he was unfortunately trapped inside an adventurous cave. The way of the adventurous cave is hidden. There is no one near to help him out. Help the Fairytale to find some useful objects and hints to escape from an adventurous cave. Good Luck and Have Fun!.
