The level assets are lost. A recreation was done for the first 2 levels to play.
Death war between you and a great army force in the world. You are having the top flight in the world. Destroy all your enemies in each level to get rewards and awards. How to play Destroy enemy flight with flight gun or bombs. Destroy enemy Ships with flight gun or bombs. Destroy enemy Island with only bombs. Finish all the enemies within given time duration to move on further levels. The remaining time will be calculated as your extra score. You have only three lives in each level. By calculating the lives you have saved in a level will be reward as a gold, sliver or bronze. Get 2000 score for Gold reward, 1500 for silver and 1000 for bronze reward.
