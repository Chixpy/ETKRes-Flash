Underground invasion is an action defense game where you can play defending Warcoder City sewers to prevent the invasion of strange creatures.

Upgrade your weapons and defenses to fight off the legion of evil creatures that attempt to destroy the protective bars that separate the city, from the sewers.

- 25 levels.
- 5 screens.
- 10 different creatures.
- Final Boss.
- 4 Weapons.
- 9 secondary weapons.
- 7 upgrades.
