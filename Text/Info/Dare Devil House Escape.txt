Dare Devil House Escape is a type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. You went out for a trip and rented a house. While you went inside a house for you was trapped inside. The door of the house is locked. You also came to know there was a devil inside that house. You want to escape from there by finding useful object, and hints. Find the right way to escape from the Dare Devil House. Good Luck Have Fun!
