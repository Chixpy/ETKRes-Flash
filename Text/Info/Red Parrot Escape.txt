Red Parrot Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game someone tried trap a red parrot in a forest by hitting it with a bow. So it was wound badly and cannot able to fly. No one is there to help the red parrot. You have to help the red parrot to escape from there by treating the wounds. Escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
