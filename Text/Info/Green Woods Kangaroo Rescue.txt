Green Woods Kangaroo Rescue is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to see the beauty of green forest. But unfortunately, you noticed that someone trapped a Kangaroo in that forest. You have to rescue the Kangaroo and find the way to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
