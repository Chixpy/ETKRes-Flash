Lemon meringue pie is a type of baked pie, usually served for dessert, made with a crust usually made of shortcrust or shortbread pastry, lemon curd filling and a fluffy meringue topping. Lemon meringue pie is typically prepared with a bottom pie crust, but with no upper crust. i.e. the meringue is directly on top of the lemon filling.Learn how to make this amazing Lemon meringue pie recipe here.
