Victorian House Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Wowescape.com. You are a house keeper of Victorian house. Today you have so many works to do. So you are doing your work till night and after finishing your work you started to your home. But when you try to open the door of your room it's not opening. You are locked inside the house. It's too late and no one is there except you. This big royal Victorian house is looking scary now. You want to escape from there to using hints and some useful objects. You need to find the key of door. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good luck. Have fun!
