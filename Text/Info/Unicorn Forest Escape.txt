Unicorn Forest Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game unfortunately a Unicorn is trapped in a fantasy world while searching for food. No one is there to help the Unicorn. You have to help the Unicorn to escape from there. Escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
