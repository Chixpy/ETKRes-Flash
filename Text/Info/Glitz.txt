1967th-GLITZ is an new escape game created by ENA Game Studio. Presume a situation there was a robbery in a museum, 20 objects were stolen. Bruter went to jewellery shop to find a object which was stolen in a museum.The shop was closed, but Bruter has a criminal idea to get into the shop. Bruter needs your help to find an object as soon as possible. Have greater excitement and fun on playing escape games.
