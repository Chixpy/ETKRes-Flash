Fiat Car Escape is a point and click escape game developed by 8BGames. Imagine that you have a vintage Fiat Car. You took your car to your car mechanic. But your mechanic asked for some spares which is not available in the market. Later you come to know some spares will be available in an abandoned car garage. Find some hidden object to solve some interesting clues to steal and escape fiat car. Good luck...Have fun!
