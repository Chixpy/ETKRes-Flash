Christmas Tree Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. In winter season everyone feeling so cool and no one wants to go out from home. But in this escape game you unfortunately trapped in a big snow land by someone. In that place the temperature is too low and also snow falling regularly . You are going to freeze in this snow falling. You need to escape from that place. Otherwise she can't be alive. Escape from there and save your life. Search for clues and objects and solve some puzzles which will help you to escape from there. Good Luck Have Fun!
