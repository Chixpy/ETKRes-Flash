Take up the turkey farm and make your business flourish in this thanksgiving time. You are running a turkey shop in the hot place of the city also with turkey farm and factory. In your turkey farm you have a special food for turkey which will make them to grow fast rapidly. Note down the customer request and make small turkey to grow rapidly by feeding it with special food. Bring it to the factory, remove the skin and bring it to the shop. Deliver it to the customer. Since it is a thanksgiving time lots of customers started coming to your shop. You are the only one person running the shop. Serve your customer within the time durations and satisfy them to come again and again. Have a fun Thanksgiving Day!
