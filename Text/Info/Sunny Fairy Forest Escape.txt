Sunny Fairy Forest Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit the Sunny Fairy Forest. But unfortunately you came to know that you are trapped inside this Sunny Fairy Forest. No one is there to help you. Escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
