Cutaway Car Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. You have a cute kitty and you love her so much and there's no doubt that cats love us as much as we love them. People who haven't been blessed with furry feline love have a difficult time believing this, though, because kitties show affection very differently than people do. But this time kitty is in trouble because she is trapped in a cutaway car, kitty is annoying to much inside there and she is feeling problem inside car. Immediate you should do something to get kitty out from there otherwise you can loss your lovable kitty. Use the tool which is around car and search for some objects which can help you to escape kitty from there. Good Luck and Have Fun!
