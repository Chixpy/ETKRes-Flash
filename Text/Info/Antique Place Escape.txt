Antique Place Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game you are an archaeologist, you went to ancient place for a research. But unfortunately you trapped inside that place. There is no one to help you out. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and by solving puzzle. Try to find the way to escape. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
