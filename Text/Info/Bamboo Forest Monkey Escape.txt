Bamboo Forest Monkey Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit bamboo forest which is located outer of the city. But you noticed that some trapped a monkey in that bamboo forest. No one is there to help him. You have to help the monkey to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
