In this game you participate in a Death tournament to free your country from the rule of an evil king. You must defeat 13 guardians of the king and the king himself. In each combat you and your opponent receive 7 tiles that represent various moves. Most of the moves require stamina, magic or both, so you must control their levels. The efficiency of the moves depends on your skills. You can improve your skills in the end of each level. You earn money in combats and can spend it to upgrade your armor and your sword or to increase your magic defense.
