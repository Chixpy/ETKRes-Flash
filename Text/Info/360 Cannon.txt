Death war between you and a great army force in the world. You are having the top 360 canon in the world. Destroy all your enemies in each level to get rewards and awards. You have to destroy enemy Soldiers, Tanks, Ships, Flights etc. Destroy enemy with different powers using your 360 canon. Finish all the enemies within given time duration to move on further levels. The remaining time will be calculated as your extra score. Upgrade your canon with your bonus score. Blast the wave after wave of enemies that are attacking in all the 360 directions.
