King Toad Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to some research in a fantasy forest. But after few hours you came to know that someone trapped the King Toad and locked in a cage. You need to help King Toad to escape from here by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
