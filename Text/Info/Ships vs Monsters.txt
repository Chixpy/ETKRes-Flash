Your kingdom is attacked by terrible sea monsters! Create a line of defense around your port and counterattack. Fight for each port and kill them in the lair (Graveyard of ships). Features of the game: - dynamic tower defense genre - 6 lines of attack\defense - 6 kinds of ships and monsters - system of stars-and-upgrades - 24 different levels - special abilities: 1) saving sailors (+coins), 2) turning the ships to broadside (attack x2), 3) shooting from fortress gun Charge the gun and fight!
