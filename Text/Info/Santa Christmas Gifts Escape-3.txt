Santa Christmas Gifts Escape-3 is a type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com . This Christmas, Santa is going to deliver gifts for seven special kids from seven different places in the world. Totally there will be 5 parts in this story. After successfully delivering 1st , 2nd and 3rd gifts Santa is going to deliver a 4th gift to a kid in this part. But his sleigh met with an accident on the way. Santa one of the reindeer is in the wound and Santa lost the gift bag and check list. Help the Santa to wound healing his reindeer and find the gift bag and check list for successfully delivering a 4th gift to a kid. Enjoy third part of this series and Have a fun!
