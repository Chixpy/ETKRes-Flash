Lovers Reunion is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. Season of love! Long time ago a couple was separated due to miss understanding between them. But they decided to forgive their mistakes and live together from this Valentine's Day. Suddendly the girl noticed that she was trapped inside her house by her parents. The door of the house is locked. Help them to escape by finding some useful objects and hints inside the house to reach her boyfriend on Valentine's Day.
