Beehive Escape is a point and click escape game developed by 8BGames. Imagine that you went to a fantasy forest as an adventurous trip. While you were enjoying your adventurous trip lonely. You found a beehive to take honey. When you went to take the necessary items to extract honey; someone locked the beehive in a cage. Find some hidden object to solve some interesting clues to escape the Beehive. Good luck...Have fun!
