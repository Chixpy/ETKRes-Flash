Haunted Forest Halloween Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit a haunted forest for your Halloween party. But unfortunately you noticed that someone trapped you inside this haunted forest. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this haunted forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
