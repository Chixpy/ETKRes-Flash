Game requires a copy of the photo you upload to be put in folder www.123unblock.com/images (this can be implemented in the future). Insert your face to put yourself into the game. Edit the size of your face in the uploaded image and click on the finish button. Keep the coffee away from the menace of the flies by thrashing the flies using swatter without hitting the coffee cup. If the flies drink the coffee fully or if you hit the coffee cup, you will lose the game. Complete each level by keeping the hot coffee away from flies up to given time duration and proceed to higher levels.
