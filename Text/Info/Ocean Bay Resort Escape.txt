Ocean Bay Resort Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to stay in a ocean bay resort for your summer holidays. While roaming around the beach you missed the way to your resort. This place is fully surrounded by water. You have to find the way to your resort by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
