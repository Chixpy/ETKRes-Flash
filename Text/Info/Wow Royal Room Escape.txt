Wow Royal Room Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Wowescape.com. Just Imagine, you are working in your royal house, today you have so many works to do so you are doing your work till night after finishing your work you started to your home but when you try to open the door of your room is not opening. You are locked inside the room. It's too late and no one is there only you are in room. This big royal room is looking scary. You want to escape from there to using hints and some useful objects. Solve the puzzles for opening the doors and escape from there. Good Luck!
