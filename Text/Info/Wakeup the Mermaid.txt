Wakeup the Mermaid is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to see the beauty of an underwater. But unfortunately, you noticed that some magical creature made Mermaid unconscious. You have to find the way to wake up the Mermaid by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
