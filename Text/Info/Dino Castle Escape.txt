Dino Castle Escape is another type of escape game developed by wowescape.com. This is story of very old time. That time in the world there is only six dinosaur remains in dinosaur species. In that time there is one king who did not want to stay dino in the planet so he catches the all six dino and locked them in a big castle cage. Now this is the interesting part of game because you have to rescue that dino from the castle and cage. First of all you want to open the door of all six cage of dino, then you have to open the main castle door. So get ready for an adventures escape game. Use your escaping mind and rescue that dino. Good Luck Have Fun!
