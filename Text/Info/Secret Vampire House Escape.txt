Secret Vampire House Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to investigate a house which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately, you came to know that house is a secret vampire's house and the door is also locked from outside. You have to find the way to escape by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
