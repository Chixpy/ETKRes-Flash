Trapped Middle Street Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. You are trapped inside in middle street room. The door of the middle street room is locked. There is no one near to help you out. You have to escape from there. Use your escaping mind and try to collect suitable objects and make use of it at appropriate places. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles and escape from the fair room. Good luck and have fun.
