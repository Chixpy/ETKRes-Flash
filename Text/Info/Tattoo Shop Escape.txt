Tattoo Shop Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. We all want to express ourselves but, we all use different ways to do it! You want to express yourself through a really chic and colorful tattoo. While come from office you went on a tattoo shop for creating tattoos on your body. It?s almost late and the owner plans to pull down the shutter of the tattoo shop. You enter the shop for a creating tattoo. The owner allows you without any regret, but after some while unaware your presence he closes the shop and moves ahead. Now you are trapped inside that tattoo shop. How will you escape now? Can you do this? Search the clues and useful objects and use them for escape from there. Good Luck Have Fun!
