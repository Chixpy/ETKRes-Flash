Love Greeting Card Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this escape game, a college going young boy prepared a greeting card to her girlfriend for this Valentine's Day. But unfortunately he was caught by his principal. And the principal took way the greeting card with him to complain about it to his parents.You need to help the young boy to find the greeting card by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
