Mysterious Cave Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you happened to came to see Mysterious Cave Forest which is located outer of the city. After few hours you decided to go back. But unfortunately you missed the way out. You have to find the way to escape by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
