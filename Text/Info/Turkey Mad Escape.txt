Turkey Mad Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. In this game one mad turkey is trapped in a house. He knows this thanksgiving the peoples are going to kill him at Thanksgiving Day so he gone mad. Now he wants to escape from his own house. You have to help the turkey and make her escape from their by finding all the clues and objects carefully. Some objects are hidden in the house. Match all the objects, find the correctly combinations and solve the puzzles with the clues. Click on the objects to interact and solve puzzles. Good luck and have fun!
