Run, jump, float, and BURP your way through 49 action-packed levels! The evil Super Fizzy Soda Company has dropped a smoke-spewing factory right in the middle of your forest, and it is your job to go kick them out! Beware though, as this is no ordinary soda factory! Avoid the spikes, lasers, scientists, fans, and more while jumping and burping your way through the factory! Be sure to collect all of the hidden collectable sodas to brag to your friends and unlock all of the hidden characters!
