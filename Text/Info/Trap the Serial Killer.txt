Trap The Serial Killer is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. So far in escape games you will be trapped inside a serial killer room and you have to escape from there. But in this escape game, a serial killer is coming to kill a baby. So you have to trap the serial in a room ,keep the door locked and save the baby. Make sure that he should not escape from there by finding useful object, and hints. Good Luck and Have Fun!
