Escape Game Save The Princess is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to see the beauty of an old castle which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately, you noticed that someone trapped the Princess in that castle. You have to find the way to rescue the Princess from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
