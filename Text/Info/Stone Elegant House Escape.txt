Stone Elegant House Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. You went out another city for office work and you stayed in a Stone Elegant house. Now your work is finished so after staying one night there, you are fully ready to go back your home. You just want to come out from that house but by mistake the house keeper locked you from outside. No one there for your help. Now what will you do? Can you escape from this stone elegant house? It is great challenge for you to escape from stone elegant house. You have to escape from here by solving the puzzles in each place. Click on objects to interact and solve puzzles. Good luck and have fun!
