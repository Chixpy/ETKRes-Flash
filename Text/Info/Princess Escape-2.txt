Prince escape tower castle is another new point and click room escape game developed by Games2rule. Story A beautiful princess was trapped by a witch in a tower castle. The witch kidnapped the princess from Flora blooms kingdom which was away from 7 mountains and 7 seas. The Princess wants to escape one bye one. The first escape was tower castle. And now the next one is blue sink sea. After escape from tower castle princess unfortunately trapped in under water sea .The Sea is called as Blue Sink Sea. Under the sea there are many mysteries and unbelievable living organism. And no one is near to help her out. You will have to use your intelligence to explore the under water for finding useful hints, objects and help her to escape from the sea. Good luck and have fun!
