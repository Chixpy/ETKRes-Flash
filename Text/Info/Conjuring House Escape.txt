Conjuring House Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you decided to buy a house. So you came to visit a house which is located outer of the city. Unfortunately your house caught fire. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this burning house by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
