Rocky Ledge Goat Rescue is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to do some research in a mountains. You noticed that someone trapped a goat and locked in cage. No one is there to help that goat. You have to rescue the goat from this mountains by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
