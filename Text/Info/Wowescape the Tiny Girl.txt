Wowescape the Tiny Girl is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Wowescape.com. In this game there are two cute tiny friends but unfortunately one tiny girl is trapped inside the house. Both are crying how the tiny girl will be escape from there? Do you want to help them? This time use your escaper mind for their cute friendship and bring out that tiny girl from the house. Search for some useful object and use them at right way, escape the girl and make them happy. Good Luck. Have fun!
