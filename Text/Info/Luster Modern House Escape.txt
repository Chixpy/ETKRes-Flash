Luster Modern House Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to stay in Luster Modern House for your holidays. But unfortunately the door is locked you from outside of the Luster Modern House. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this Luster Modern House by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
