Wow Untidy Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Wowescape.com. In this game you are trapped inside an untidy room. You have to escape from there by finding the objects and solving puzzles. As we know the room is untidy so you have to use your sharp eyes to finding clues. Can you escape from there? Test your escaping skill and try to escape from there in less time. Good luck and have fun!
