Easter Bunny Room Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you came to a Easter Bunny Room for Easter day. After a long time you came to know that you are trapped inside that Easter Bunny Room. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
