Genie Lamp Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, someone stolen the Genie lamp and locked in his house. This is the right time for you to get into the house and get back the Genie lamp. You have to find the way to escape by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
