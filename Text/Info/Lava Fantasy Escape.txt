Lava Fantasy Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you came out for a trip to visit lava fantasy world. But unfortunately you missed the way out. You are fully surrounded by lava. No one is there to help you out. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
