Escape Games: The Nerd is the 704th point and click online escape game from 123bee.com. A group of student plan to make a fun of a nerdy student by locking him inside a house. The students decide to invite him to a house by saying that they have arranged a party for him. As per the plan, he comes to the house with more expectations, but the students lock him inside the house and leave. Help the nerd man to escape from the house by searching for the key.
