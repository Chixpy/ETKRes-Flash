Basement Garage Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit Basement Garage to get some parts for your car. But unfortunately someone trapped you inside the Basement Garage. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
