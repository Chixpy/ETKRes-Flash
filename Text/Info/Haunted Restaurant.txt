Haunted restaurant is running by a witch. The witch having a problem, she lost her magic power slightly. She wants to regain her power back, for that witch has to make hundreds of haunted foods with her small magic power to bring back her full magic power. Help the witches to complete the task within the time duration. Unlock the food one by one and go further stages to complete the game. Bring back the magic power to the witch to run haunted restaurant greatly.
