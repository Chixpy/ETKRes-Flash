Bamboo Forest Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com. You are looking for some adventure and decided to go for rowing alone in to the bamboo forest. After reaching some distance you see some beautiful places. After taking rest you decided to go back but unfortunately you lost your way. Now you are far away from city. So no one there for your help. You are alone between those big bamboo trees. Day is going to end. You are hearing some scary noises there! You want to escape from this place soon before the day ends. Find your way to escape quickly! But before that you have to search some clues, objects and solving some puzzles then you can find your oar. So start searching and escape soon from there. Good luck and have fun!
