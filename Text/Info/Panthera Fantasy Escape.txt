Panthera Fantasy Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this fantasy escape game, you came to know that someone trapped a panthera and locked in a cage. They also changed the panthera into a small kitty using a magic wand. Now you have to rescue the panthera back again and escape from that cage by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
