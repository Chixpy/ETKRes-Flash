New Escape Rooms is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. You are decided to buy house. You went with your family members to buy a house at nearby city. Once you entered the house the manager of that apartment asked you to take a look into their sample house. While seeing their sample house main door got lock unfortunately. Try to escape from there. Find the useful objects and clues. Solve the entire puzzle and escape from there. Good luck and have fun!
