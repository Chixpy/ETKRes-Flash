Zoo Escape is an interesting escape game for escape game lover presented by games2rule.com. In this game one day you went to the nearby zoo along with your friends. But unfortunately your friends forget you and leave you inside the zoo. The zoo in-charge person locked the main gate and went off without knowing that you are roaming inside. No one is there to help the boy. So you have to help the boy to escape from there. Try to get out the boy with the help of zoo objects and animals. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve some interesting puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
