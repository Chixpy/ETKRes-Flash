Fancy Vintage House Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to stay in a Fancy Vintage House for your vocation. But unfortunately the door is locked from outside of the Fancy Vintage House. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this Fancy Vintage House by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
