Conference Hall Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. Today is conference in your office. After finishing the conference you fell asleep. Suddenly everyone in the office disappeared and you were left alone in conference hall. How you will feel? Later you came to know that you're locked inside the office and could not find ways to escape from there. Now what will you do because no one at office for your help and you have to escape from there. All you need to do is there are few hidden objects and hints which will help you to escape from the office without any trouble. Search and find them for escape from your office conference hall. Good Luck Have fun!
