1.You will have to approach customers to get their orders (You have to click on the table to know what the customer wants). 2.Then you have to go back to the chef to place the order (You have to click on the chef to place the order). 3.Once the order is ready you will have click on it. 4.You should then take the order to the customer to get paid. 5.If you place the wrong order you will have throw it in the dustbin. 6.When you throw food in the dustbin you lose money. 7.If you reach the target amount you can proceed to the next level.
