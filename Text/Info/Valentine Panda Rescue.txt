Valentine Panda Rescue is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to see the beauty of a forest during the Valentine's day. But unfortunately you noticed that someone trapped a female panda in a cage. And male panda seeking your help. You have to rescue the female panda from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
