Great Office Escape is a type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com In this game, you worked for long hours throughout the night. After finishing your work you came to know that you are trapped inside the office. How your mind will work if you are really trapped in such a place. No one is there to help you. Use presence of mind to utilize the objects found in the place to make an escape from there. Good luck and have fun!
