New Year 2019 Escape is a point and click escape game developed by 8BGames. Today is a New Year Eve and you invited your friends for night party in your estate. So you went early to do the preparations. After finishing the preparation, you noticed that you misplaced the entrance key. You have to open the door before your friends arrives. Find some hidden object to solve some interesting clues to find the key to celebrate New Year. Good luck...Have fun!
