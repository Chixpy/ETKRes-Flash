Halloween Pumpkin Adventure Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit a adventure place on Halloween day. But unfortunately you noticed that someone trapped a Halloween Pumpkin. No one is there to help him. You have to help him to escape from this adventure place by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles.Good Luck Have Fun!
