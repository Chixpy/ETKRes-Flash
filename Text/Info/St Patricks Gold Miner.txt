Use the claw and reel to take gold coins, rocks, snail and worm out of the earth. The claw swings back and forth to grab something. Claw lowers to grab things and reel it up. Heavy objects like rocks and large gold coin will be harder to reel up. You can get more money by collecting gold coins. The other things will fetch you little amount of money. . Collect the target amount of money before the time runs out and enters the successive levels or else the game will get over.
