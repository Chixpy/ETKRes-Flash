Horse Barn Escape Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit Horse Barn to do some research about horses. But unfortunately you missed the way out. This place looks very scary. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
