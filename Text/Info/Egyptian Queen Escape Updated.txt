Egyptian Queen Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this fantasy escape game, you came to visit a pyramid for your research work which is located outer of the Egypt. Unfortunately someone trapped the Queen of this Egyptian pyramid. No one is there to help the princess. Now you have to help the princess to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
