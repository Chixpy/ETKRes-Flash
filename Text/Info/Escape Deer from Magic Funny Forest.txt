Escape Deer from Magic Funny Forest is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game a deer was in deep sleep. Suddenly some superficial power trapped the deer into a magic forest. She cannot even able to stand in that place. She feeling afraid and want to escape from that place. But there is no one to help you. You have to help the deer to escape from that magic fantasy forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
