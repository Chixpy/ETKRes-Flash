Escape from Japan Suicide Forest is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. Each year the authorities remove so many bodies found hanging at this suicide forest. Exactly why so many choose to end their lives in the forest remains something of a mystery. In this game you to wish to find the mystery of this suicide forest.So you came to the forest and spent some times there but unfortunately you missed the way out. No one is there to help you out. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
