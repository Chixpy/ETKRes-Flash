Green Box Room Escape is a another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com In this escape game a green box is unfortunately trapped in a 9 rooms of an apartment and the door is locked of every room. There is no one in the apartment to help the green box to escape. So help the green box to escape from that apartment by using some objects from there. You can escape step by step only. So don?t miss the objects to collect before you go to further step otherwise you have to fall again into the previous step and collect the objects than play the same steps again. Have a fun.
