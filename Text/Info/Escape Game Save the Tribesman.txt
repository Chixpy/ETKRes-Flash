Escape Game Save the Tribesman is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to see the beauty of a greeny forest. But unfortunately you noticed that someone trapped a Tribesman. No one is there to help him. You have to find the way to save the Tribesman by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle.
