Zombies Attack Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit a forest which is located outer of your city. But unfortunately the forest is full of zombies. And they all trying to attack you. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this zombies forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
