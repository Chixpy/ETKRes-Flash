Quicksand Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. In a dense forest, a jungle girl was trapped in quicksand there is no one near to help her. So use your escaping skill to help her from this tragedy. All the needed objects are hidden somewhere in that place. Find them and help the girl to get out from the quicksand. Escape the girl and enjoy the game.Good Luck!
