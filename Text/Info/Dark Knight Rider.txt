Batman rises again for a good cause! Eight years on, a new terrorist leader, Bane, overwhelms Gotham's finest, and the Dark Knight resurfaces to protect a city that has branded him an enemy. Enemies are travelling in the tanks and container. Destroy them and protect the city. The game is not as easy as it looks, you will see that is very difficult to avoid hitting or shooting other cars. Take a ride with our batman and have fun!
