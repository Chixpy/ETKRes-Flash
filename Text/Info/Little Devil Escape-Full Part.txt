Little Devil Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. This is a new escape game called as little devil escape. In this little devil escape game one little girl is trapped in a devil house and no one at the house for help the girl to escape from there. She needs someone helps to escape from that danger devil house. So you decided to help the girl and went to that house but when you enter there the door is locked. Now you are also trapped on that devil house. In this game you have to exit the ghost from little girl to find the key of door and escape from the house. So start searching some clues and objects and solve some scary puzzle. Good Luck. Have a fun.
