Feed the wild is fun filled arcade style game where the objective is to feed the food to the animals. It is highly entertaining, with players competing to see who can able to feed the animals in few moves. The goal in �Feed the wild� is fairly simple, the user has to do is shoot the food to over the hungry animals depending upon what they eat and make it sleep. The game starts off fairly easy and gets more challenging after some levels. For every mistake made the satisfication meter gets reduced, however it can also be replenished with the correct food. Use the left and right arrow keys to control the cloud shooter. Use the space bar to fire food. Use the up arrow key to choose the correct food. Full fill the satisfactions meter to complete the level.
