Escape Game Save the Pregnant Girl is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to know that a pregnant girl is trapped inside a house. But unfortunately, she also got a delivery pain. No one is there to help her. You have to find the way to rescue her and take her to the hospital by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
