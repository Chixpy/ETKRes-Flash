Zombies Abandoned Graveyard Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to do some research in abandoned graveyard. But unfortunately this place is surrounded by full of zombies. This place also looks very scary. You have to find the way to escape from this graveyard by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
