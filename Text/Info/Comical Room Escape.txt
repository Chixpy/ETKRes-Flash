Comical Room Escape Comical room escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com. In this escape game you are locked inside a comical room. There are the entire thing is looking comical. You have to escape from there. You want to find the available objects kept around the place. Use those objects at appropriate places and make use of them. Click on those objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles and escape from that comical room. Good Luck. Have a fun.
