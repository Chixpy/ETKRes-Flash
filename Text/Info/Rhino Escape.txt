Rhino Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit a forest which is located outer of the city. Unfortunately someone trapped a Rhino in this forest. No one is there to help this Rhino. You have to help Rhino to escape from this forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
