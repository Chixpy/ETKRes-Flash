OBJECTIVE
=========

explore the desert. discover the secrets of the orbs and the ruins.
find all four endings!

carried items will be altered after 10s exposure to sunlight.

CONTROLS
=========

LEFT / RIGHT / UP / DOWN - move and climb
SPACE - jump
Z - drop item
-/+ adjust volume
