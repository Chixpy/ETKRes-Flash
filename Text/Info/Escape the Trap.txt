Escape the trap is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com. In this game you are living alone for job far from your family. One day you are trapped inside your own house and no one near to you because you are living alone. You have to escape yourself from that room. Show your sharpens of mind and escaping skill search some useful object use them at right way and solve some puzzles for escape from there. Good Luck.
