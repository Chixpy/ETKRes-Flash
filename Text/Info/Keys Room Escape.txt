Keys Room Escape is a type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. You went out another city for office work and you stayed in a hotel. Now your work is finished so after staying one night there, you are fully ready to go back your home. You just want to come out from your hotel room but by mistake the hotel staff locked you from outside. No one there for your help. Now what will you do? You have to go back office soon and you are trapped here. Can you escape from this Key room? It is great challenge for you to escape from key room of the hotel. You have to escape from here by solving the puzzles in each place. Click on objects to interact and solve puzzles. Good luck and have fun!
