Jungle Explorer Escape is another type of escape game developed by wowescape.com. In this game one explorer girl went to a jungle to explore about wild life. She went far inside from jungle. Suddenly some tribal people come and catch that girl. They taken her for cook. So the tribal people put the girl in a big port and leave there a snake as a guard. The explorer girl is fully scared. She is counting her last time. How she will be come out from there? She wants someone?s help for escape from there and save her life. Would you like to help her? Just help that innocent explorer girl by rescue from there. First of all you have to kill the guard snake from there and then you can rescue the girl. Use your clever mind and search some clues, objects and solve some puzzle for escape her from there. Good Luck Have fun!
