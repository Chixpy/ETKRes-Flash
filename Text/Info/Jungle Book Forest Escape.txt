Jungle Book Forest Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. This game is based on the movie The Jungle Book. In this game someone trapped the Mowgli deep inside the forest. With the help of Mowgli's friends help the Mowgli to escape from that place by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
