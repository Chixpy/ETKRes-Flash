Snow World Escape is a type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. In this winter session you went for celebrating your holidays before Christmas in a snow world hills station. While you reached there , weather was very good but after one day suddenly it started heavy snow falling and every roads and ways are blocked from snow. Now you are trapped in a snow world. How your mind will work if you are really trapped in such a so cool place. Use presence of mind to utilize the objects found in the place to make an escape from there and go back your home for celebrating Christmas. Good luck and have fun!
