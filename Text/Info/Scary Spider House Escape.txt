Halloween Costume Escape 2016 is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit a spider house for Halloween party. But unfortunately you are trapped inside in this spider house. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this scary spider house by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles.Good Luck Have Fun!
