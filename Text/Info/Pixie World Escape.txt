Pixie World Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit an fantasy world. In this world you came to know that someone trapped a girl pixie. No one is there to help her. You have to help the pixie from this fantasy world by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
