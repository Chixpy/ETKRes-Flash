Wow Abandoned Hotel Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this escape game you came see a abandoned hotel where you stayed there when you was young. After few minutes you decided to move out. But you came know that some trapped you inside that abandoned hotel. There is no one to help you out. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
