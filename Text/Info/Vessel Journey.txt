1966th-A Vessel Journey is an new point and click type escape game developed by ENA Game Studio. Presume a situation there was a robbery in a museum, 20 objects were stolen. Bruter went to harbour area to find a binocular in a ship.Unfortunately, the bridge was broken.Bruter needs your help to set the path to get in the ship. Collect objects and solve interesting puzzles. Have fun on playing escape games.
