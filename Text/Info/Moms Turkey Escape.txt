Moms Turkey Escape is a point and click room escape game developed by GAMES2RULE.COM. For a thanksgiving dinner, mom locked a turkey in a cage. You want to save the turkey by escaping it. In a room there are two doors one is front door and another is back door. The back door is locked; you want to open that to escape. Because front door front is living room mom will be there. So the only escape way for turkey is the back door. You have to scare the cat to run away from the turkey. Happy Thanksgiving Day!
