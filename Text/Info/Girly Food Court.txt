Get ready to build your own dream restaurant once again! A cute girl is running a food court in the city. Lots of customers started coming to her shop. Now you have to help the girl to serve the customer. Give the customers their desired food, do not make them wait for long time, waiting time will be indicated, serve them before that or they will leave the shop. The number of food items will increase in the subsequent levels, and limit will also increase. Good Luck !
