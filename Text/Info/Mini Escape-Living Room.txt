Mini Escape-Living Room is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. In this game you are trapped inside a mini house. In that house there is couple of room. The door was closed by somebody from outside. So, you have to escape from there. Find the door key. You have to search for clues, Objects inside mini house and then solve some interesting puzzles for finding the unlocking door key. Use your best escaping skills to escape from that house. Good luck and have fun!
