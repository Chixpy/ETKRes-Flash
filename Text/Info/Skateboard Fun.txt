Help this boy to skate well and get points by achieving the target before the time runs out.Earn points for every stunt he does. Go higher and higher so that you get bonus points for hitting the stars.Achieve the target fast to save the time for the next levels. The time you save on each level, will be added to the next level. If you lose the game, you lose 500 points. Get more points by pressing X and C stunt keys.
