Honeymoon Room Escape is a new point and click escape game developed by Games2rule.com. You are just newly married and went Japan for your honeymoon and staying in a beautiful room of resort. But after one night you found yourself locked in your room. There is no one near to help you out and unfortunately the room service phone is also not working. It's a great challenge for you because you have to back your country also. If you didn?t escape soon from there then you will be miss your flight. So apply your logic and find some useful objects, hints and solve some puzzles to escape from the room. Good Luck Have Fun!
