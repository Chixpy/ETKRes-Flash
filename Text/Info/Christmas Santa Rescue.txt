Christmas Santa Rescue is another point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. This time Santa is in trouble. This Christmas Santa is going to deliver gifts for kids. He started his journey and delivering gift for kids. But unfortunately Santa and his reindeers were trapped by a unknown person. Santa needs your help to rescue from there. So help the Santa to escape from there. Use your mind to escape Santa from there and deliver the gift successfully to the kids. Enjoy and Have Fun!
