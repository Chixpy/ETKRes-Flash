After being the leader of a nationally known paranormal investigation team, you decided to go out on your own. After several solo investigations that didn't turn up much, you were alerted of a haunted asylum on the south side of the city. You decided to go there on your own and see what you could find. After exploring the building for a few hours you started hearing noises. Then everything went black. Now that you are awake, it's time to search for some answers.
