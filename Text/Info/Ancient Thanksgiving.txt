Ancient thanksgiving is a type of point and click escape game developed by GAMES2RULE.COM In this game we are going to see how the ancient tribes celebrated their Thanksgiving Day. Imagine you are in that period now. Normally they will select a man from their group and send will him alone to hunt the turkey during Thanksgiving Day. Now this time they selected you to hunt the giant turkey. And the tribes also given a hint about the hunting method and what are the objects to be useful to you. Once you collect all the objects you will be redirect to the forest to hunt the turkey. And then celebrate the ancient type Thanksgiving Day.
