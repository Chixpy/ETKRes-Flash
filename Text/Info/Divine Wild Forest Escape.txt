Divine Wild Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit a divine wild forest to do some research work. But unfortunately you missed the way out of divine wild forest. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this divine wild forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle.
