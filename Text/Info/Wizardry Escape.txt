Wizardry Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit a wizardry forest which is located outer of the city. In that wizardry forest you came to know that someone trapped a little girl in a cage. No one is there to help her. You have to help the little girl to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
