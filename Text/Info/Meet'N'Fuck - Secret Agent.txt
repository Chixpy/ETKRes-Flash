Today you will feel like a secret agent. Terrorists are planning another attack on the United States. They've forced famous inventor Professor Emmett Von Braun to build a machine for creation of a murderous clone army! Our undercover agents were able to free Professor Von Braun and now we know that the 5 parts of the machine are hidden by his lovers around Europe. You have to find these parts and bring them to the Bureau. Good luck!
