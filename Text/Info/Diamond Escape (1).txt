Diamond Escape is a type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com In this game, someone planning to steal your diamond tonight. You need to take the diamind the leave the house. But unfortunately you missed your keys. How your mind will work if you are really trapped in such a place. No one is there to help you. Use presence of mind to utilize the objects found in the place to make an escape from there. Good luck and have fun!
