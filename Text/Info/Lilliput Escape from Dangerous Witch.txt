Lilliput Escape from Dangerous Witch is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, a dangerous witch prisoned an Lilliput in her house. The witch house looks very scary. No one is there to help Lilliput. You have to help Lilliput to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
