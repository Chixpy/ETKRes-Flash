Psycho escape is a type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. You are trapped inside a psycho room which is outside of the city by one of your enemy. This room is specially made for torturing the peoples. That room is looking very dangerous and like an old subway. Floor and wall of the room is blood. It's seems like so many people's are killed here before. There are so many objects for torturing people. Before the psycho comes there, you have to escape from that dangerous torturing house. Save your life and think something for escape from there. It's about your life so cleverly escape from that psycho house. Search for some clues and objects which will be help you to escape and use then at correct way and escape from there. Good Luck Have Fun.
