IT Company Trapped Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you are working in a IT company. Due to some important work, you worked till late night. After finishing your work you decided to move. But unfortunately, someone locked you inside the office. You have to find the way to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
