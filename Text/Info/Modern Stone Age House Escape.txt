Modern Stone Age House Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to stay in modern stone age house. But unfortunately someone locked the door from outside of the modern stone age house. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this modern stone age house by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
