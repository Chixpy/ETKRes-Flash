In this game an undertaker has discovered the bare bones skeleton of a man on a mysterious island. Due to his undertaking duties, he has decided to bury the body. Before he can do this, something stands in his way... how can you bury a body without the bones?! Using the different elements of the environment see if you can give this bag of bones the burial he deserves! He's already waited long enough for it; don't make his wait any longer!
