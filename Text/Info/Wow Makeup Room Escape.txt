Wow makeup room escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. In this game you are unfortunately trapped by mistake inside a high class super star celebrity makeup room. You want to open the room door but it's locked from outside. The celebrity makeup room is very highly decorated and full of makeup objectives. No one there for open the door. Now it's challenge for you. You have to escape anyhow from there by using clue and makeup objects which is inside that room. Show your escaping skill and escape from that room. Good Luck. Have a fun.
