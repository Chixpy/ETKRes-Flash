In this game your task is to match the colorful butterflies and put them into the bottle to win the game. First identity the same color butterflies then moves the arrow on it and connects the pair without intersecting with other butterflies and also collects the single butterflies.  After paring just give a click to put them in to the bottle. You have 3 lives in this game. You need to finish the target within the given time. For collecting butterflies your score will increase on your scoreboard.
