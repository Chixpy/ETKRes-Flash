Serve the requested food rapidly to the family members before the time ends. Use mouse to interact. To serve the requested food to family member, click on the requested food from the set of food items and release the mouse click on the appropriate platter.  If you fail to serve the requested food within the time limit, you will lose a life. Complete the level successfully to unlock the other levels. To mute the sound, click the mute button.  To bring the game to pause, click the pass button.
