Christmas Church Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you planned you go for a Christmas party after your prayer in Church. But someone trapped you inside the Church and locked the door from outside. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from there with his gifts by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
