Refuge Escaping is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. For some security reason someone locked you far from city inside a refuge house but now you have to go out from that house. What will you do? Who lock you that person is not there and no one near to your help. You want to escape yourself from there. Think and use your escaper mind. Search for some clues, objects and use them for escape. Good luck Have fun.
