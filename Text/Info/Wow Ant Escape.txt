Wow Ant Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game someone trapped an ant made him unconscious in a forest. No one is there to help the ant. You have to first make the ant conscious and have to help him to escape from there. Escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
