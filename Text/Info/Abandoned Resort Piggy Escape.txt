Abandoned Resort Piggy Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this escape game you came to see a abandoned resort where you stayed there when you was young. After few minutes you move out. But you left your pet piggy inside that abandoned resort. You came to know that some trapped your piggy inside that abandoned hotel. You have to help the piggy to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
