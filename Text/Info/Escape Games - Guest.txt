Escape Games: The Guest is the 713th point and click online escape game from 123bee.com. The people at home get a call from the relative that a girl is coming to their house to stay for some days. But, they go out by giving the key to the neighbor and informing them that their relative will arrive in an hour. Later, the girl arrives and gets the key from the neighbor. She enters into a wrong house and the door gets locked. Now, the girl has to escape from the house before the owner arrives. Help the girl by searching for the spare key.
