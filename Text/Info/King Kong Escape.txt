King Kong Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. One pleasant evening someone attacked King Kong and trapped him in a steel cage. They also kidnapped his girl friend to the city. Now King Kong is unconscious. No one is there to help him. So you have to help the King Kong to escape from that cage and also to help him to the way of city by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
