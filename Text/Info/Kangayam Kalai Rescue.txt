Kangayam Kalai Rescue is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you were having a pet bull named Kangayam Kalai. But some of your enemies kidnapped the bull from your house and trapped in a cage. You have to rescue your Kangayam Kalai from this place by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
