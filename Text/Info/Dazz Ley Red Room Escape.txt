Dazz Ley Red Room Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this escape game someone trapped Dazz Ley inside a red room. There is no one to help Dazz Ley out. You have to help Dazz Ley to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
