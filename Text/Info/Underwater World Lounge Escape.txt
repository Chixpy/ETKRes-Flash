Underwater World Lounge Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you came to Underwater World Lounge for your holidays . But unfortunately you was trapped inside that Underwater World Lounge. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
