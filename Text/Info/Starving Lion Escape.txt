Starving Lion Escape is a point and click escape game developed by 8BGames. Imagine that you went to your forest house on a vacation. As you haven't went there for a long time. When you went there you saw a lion trapped inside a cage. You don't know how long it's been trapped in it. The lion must be very hungry. Find some hidden object to solve some interesting clues to fetch the flesh and bone for Lion before it starves to death. Good luck...Have fun!!
