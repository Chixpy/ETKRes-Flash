Cupboard House Escape is a another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com. You are trapped in a fully furnished cupboard house and no one in the house for helping you. In this house all rooms are fully furnished with cupboard and your escaping clue may be hidden in this cupboard. Can you escape from this complex cupboards house. Use your presence of mind, search every small and big clue, use objects in the right way and escape from that house. Good luck. Have a fun!
