*********************************** This is part 1 of a 5 part series!! ********************************** You decided to travel around the world in your one man canoe. After 23 days in the open sea, you ran into a gnarly storm. Your canoe was tipped over several times and you lost everything except your trusty pocket knife. You noticed your canoe was taking on water so you were forced to stop at Spooky Island. Can you survive and escape? Good luck and have fun playing!
