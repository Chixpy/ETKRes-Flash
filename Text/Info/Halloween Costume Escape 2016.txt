Halloween Costume Escape 2016 is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, few friends locked a girl inside her house and also hidden her Halloween dress. This have to go for Halloween party but its getting late. No one is there to help her. You have to rescue the girl from this place by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
