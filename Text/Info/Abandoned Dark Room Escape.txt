Abandoned Dark Room Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. You came to see your old mansion rooms after some long years, you spent some time there and decide to move home. But the door of the abandoned rooms is locked from outside. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Try to find the key of the door. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
