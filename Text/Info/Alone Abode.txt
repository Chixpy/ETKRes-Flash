1963rd-ALONE ABODE is an interesting point and click type new escape game developed by ENA Game Studio. Presume a situation there was a robbery in a museum, 20 objects were stolen. There was a house in the outerside of the city and the thief rarely go to that house. One day he hides the tusk inside the house which was stolen in a museum. Bruter went to that house to find a tusk. Have fun on playing escape games.
