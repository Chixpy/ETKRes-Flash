Devils Land Escape is point and click new escape game developed by Games 2 Rule.com. Some devils have kidnapped you and taken you in their world and leaves on their land. Unexpectedly the devils are disappeared from there. After waiting for a long time, you decided to escape from the devils land. There are so many scary objects but any how you have to escape from there. Use special skills to escape from there. Touch and sense the objects for a hint and solve some scary puzzle for escape from there. Best of luck. Have a fun.
