No Escape from Halloween Room is another new point and click escape game from games2rule.com. Halloween festival started. In this game you came to visit a halloween room which is located at outer of the city. But unfortunately you was trapped inside that room. No one is there to help you out. This place look very scary. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
