Desolate Trip Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com. A don't miss game for all adventure lovers! You and your friends decided for an adventures trip to a desolate place. While enjoying, your friends unexpectedly get lost in the midst of the desolate mountains and your group forgot you. Now you are in trouble, no one there till so far. This place looks haunted. You have to escape from this place before something miserably happened. Find a way to escape quickly! Good luck and have fun!
