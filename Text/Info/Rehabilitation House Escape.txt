Rehabilitation House Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to stay in Rehabilitation House for your holidays. But unfortunately the door is locked you from outside of the Rehabilitation House. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this Rehabilitation House by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
