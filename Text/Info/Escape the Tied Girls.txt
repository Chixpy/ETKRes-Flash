Escape the Tied Girls is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. A couple of thieves hijacked three girls and locked them inside their office room. All girls? hands and mouth were tied and the door of the office room is locked. There is no one near to help you out. Help the girls to find some useful objects and hints to escape from the room as well as from the thieves. Good Luck and Have Fun!
