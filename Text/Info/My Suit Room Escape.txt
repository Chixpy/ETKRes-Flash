My Suit Room Escape is another new point and click escape game developed by wowescape.com. During this festive season you are out of country for celebrating the holidays and staying in a beautiful luxuries suit room. But after one night you lose the key of your room and you are locked inside of room. So you are trapped in the room and there is no communication way to inform the hotel staff. Also there is no one near to help you out. It's a great challenge for you. Find the alternative key which is hidden somewhere in your room but before that you have to solve some puzzle and you have to go through some other objects so first of all finds some clues, objects and use them for finding the key which is hidden inside your room. Apply your logic and find the key of room and escape from there. Good Luck Have Fun!
