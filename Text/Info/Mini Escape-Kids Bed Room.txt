Mini Escape Kids- Bed Room is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. one day you went to the house of your friend due to vacation, you guys planned to play a game at the room of your friend's house, after a long time you slept their because of tiredness, without knowing this your friend locked the room and went out. Once you woke up you tried many time to unlock but you can't, so search for the clue inside the room and try to escape with those clues. Click on the objects to interact and solve puzzles. Good luck and have fun!
