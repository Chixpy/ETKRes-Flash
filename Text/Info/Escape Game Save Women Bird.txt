Escape Game Save The Women Bird is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to see the beauty of a fantasy forest which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately, you noticed that someone trapped a women bird in a cage. You have to find the way to save the women bird from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
