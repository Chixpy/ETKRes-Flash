Abandoned Temple Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game you came to visit a abandoned temple which is located at outer of the city. But unfortunately you missed the way out. No one is there to help you out. The place look very scary. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
