Alien Mushroom Forest Escape is another new point and click escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you are in a fantasy world named Alien mushroom forest. Some trapped you inside this forest. You don't know to escape from that place. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this alien mushroom forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
