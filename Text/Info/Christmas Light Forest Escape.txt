Christmas Light Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to a light forest to celebrate Christmas which is located outer of the city. But unfortunately, you came to know that someone trapped Reindeer from Santa. You have to help Santa to find out the Reindeer by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
