Escape Game:Save the Ladybird is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this escape game someone trapped a ladybird in his garden. But the ladybird need to go to the forest and live with her family. You have to help the ladybird to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
