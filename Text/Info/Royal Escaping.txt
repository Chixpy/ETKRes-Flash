Royal Escaping is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com. You are working in your royal house, today you have so many works to do so. You are doing your work till night, after finishing your work you started to your home but when you try to open the door of palace is not opening. You are locked inside the royal house. It's too late and no one is there only you are in room. You want to escape from there to using hints and some useful objects. Solve the puzzles for opening the doors and escape from there. Good Luck!
