New Angry Birds Escape 2016 is another new point and click escape game from games2rule.com. In this imaginary game, some trapped the new angry birds and locked inside the cage. They don't know to how to escape from that cage. You have to help that new angry birds to escape from that cage by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
