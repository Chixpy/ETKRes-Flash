Glow Island Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game someone trapped you to a fantasy world name glow island. You don't know how you came here. But this place look very scary. No one is there to you help you out. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Try to find the key of the door. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
