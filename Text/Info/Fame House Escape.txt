Fame House Escape is another new point and click escape game developed by games2rule.com. You are working in a fame house. Today you have so many works to do, so you are doing your work till night. After finishing your work you started to your home. While trying to open the door you came to know that you are locked inside the room. It's too late and no one is there around the room. This fame house is looking scary at the dark night. You want to escape from there to using hints and some useful objects. Solve the puzzles for opening the doors and escape from there. Good Luck!
