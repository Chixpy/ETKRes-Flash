Abandoned Island Easter Bunny Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit the Abandoned Island. But unfortunately you came to know a bunny is trapped inside this Abandoned Island. No one is there to help the bunny. Help the bunny to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
