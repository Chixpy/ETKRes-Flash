After Christmas and New Year Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. This is a new and fantasy type escape game. Where you are trapped in your own place where after Christmas and New Year the time is stuck and environment is same as Christmas and New Year. Still everything is same like that. You have to make environment normal and correct. Use your sharp escaping mind and search some objects, clues and solve some puzzle for make the environment as well as normal. Good Luck Have a fun.
