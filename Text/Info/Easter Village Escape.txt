Easter Village Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this Easter someone trapped bunnies and some eggs in a village. The door of the village is locked from outside. You have to the bunnies to escape with eggs from there by finding useful scary object hints and solving puzzle. Try to find the key of the door. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Happy Easter. Good Luck Have Fun! .
