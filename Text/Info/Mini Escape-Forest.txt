Mini Escape-Forest is a type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. In this game you are trapped inside a forest castle. There is outside of forest big wall and only one door for escape from there. The door is closed by somebody from outside. So, you have to escape from that forest castle. Find the main door key. You have to search for clues, objects and solve some interesting and puzzles for finding the unlocking door key. Use your best escaping skills to escape from that forest castle. Good luck and have fun!
