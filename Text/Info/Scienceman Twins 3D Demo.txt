Another tech demo I made where you can move in faux-3d space and shoot at or jump on a bunch of raccoons! Play as the Sciencemen, Trigona, the Snuhbee, or me!

CONTROLS
Press WASD to move
Press UP, LEFT, RIGHT, or DOWN to shoot
Press SPACE to jump
Press 1 to change characters
Press R to restart

Demo made using HaxeFlixel.
