Tobe’s Great Escape is part 2 of the original Tobe’s Vertical Adventure spin off.
Yet again trapped in a crumbling cave, Tobe must try his best to escape. Without additional items and power up to aid him here, Tobe has to make the great escape with his wall maneuver, running and jumping from all over, while collecting treasures and rescuing animals. Run as far as you can, in this addictive endless platformer.
