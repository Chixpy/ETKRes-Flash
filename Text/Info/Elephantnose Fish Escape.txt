Elephantnose Fish Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came under the sea to see its beauty. But unfortunately, you noticed that someone trapped an Elephantnose Fish in a cage. You have to find the way to rescue the Elephantnose Fish from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles.
