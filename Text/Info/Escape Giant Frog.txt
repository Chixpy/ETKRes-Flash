Escape Giant Frog is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you came to see the beauty of fantasy forest. But unfortunately someone trapped a giant frog inside this fantasy forest. No one is there to help that giant frog. You have to help the giant frog to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
