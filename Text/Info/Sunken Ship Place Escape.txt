Sunken Ship Place Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game you came under the sea with your friends for a research of sunken ship. After few hours to came to know that you are trapped inside the sea. Others left the place and no one is there to help you. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Try to find the key of the door. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
