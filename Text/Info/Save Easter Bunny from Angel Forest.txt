Save Easter Bunny from Angel Forest is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this escape game someone trapped a Easter bunny in a Angels forest and also made Angels into statue. There is no one to rescue the Easter bunny and also the Angels. You have to help the Easter bunny to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
