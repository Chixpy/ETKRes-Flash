Throne Rooms Escape is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game you came to visit a throne rooms. But unfortunately you missed the way out and also a little trapped inside there. No one is there to help you out. You have to escape from there and also save the little girl by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
