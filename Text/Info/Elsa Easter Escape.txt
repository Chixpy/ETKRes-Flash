Elsa Easter Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. This is a brand new escape game for wowescape fans. The story of this game is related to the snow queen Elsa. One Easter day while she was walking on a iceland and she unfortunately lost her way. Now she is trapped alone in iceland. To return back into the castle she needs a key to open the door. The snow queen needs someone?s help to go back her castle. You have to help the Elsa to return to castle. Search some useful clues, object and extract them with puzzle to escape the Elsa from that place. Good Luck Have Fun!
