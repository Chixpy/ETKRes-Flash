Recost is an Intense Action Platform Shooter it has 20 different weapons, 8 different Maps with their own Boss battles, 2 types of Missions "Campaign" and "Zombies" with the Player's ability to upgrade soldier's Health and Equipment including buying different kinds of weapons from Rifles to Machine guns while you shoot your way against hundreds of enemy grunts, zombies and land to aerial vehicles and also Power ups that increases the Player's ability to play the game more satisfying with an in game obstacles that lets you think hard on how you maneuver in game battle.
