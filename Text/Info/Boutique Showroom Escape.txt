Boutique Showroom Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this escape game, you came to an Boutique showroom to some buy dresses. After back from the trial room you noticed that Boutique showroom is closed from outside. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this Boutique showroom by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
