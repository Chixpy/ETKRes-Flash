En kort svartvit flashfilm om en svartvit gubbe som bland annat blir träffad av en sten i huvudet. Se den! Eftersom den inte är så lång så har du säkert tid.

---

A short flash movie about a man that is hit by a stone in the head. Watch it! It's very short so you probably have the time.

At the end the man asks if you want to watch it again. If you pick NEJ (meaning no) he will say good bye and fade away. If you pick JA (meaning yes) he will ask you to wait while he put everything back the way it was, and then the whole thing is repeated again.
