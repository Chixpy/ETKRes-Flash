Thanksgiving Idle Forest House Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit a forest outside of your town for this Thanksgiving party. But unfortunately you missed the way out of this forest. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
