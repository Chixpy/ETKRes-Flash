Mysterious Turkey Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to visit an mysterious turkey forest for some research work. But unfortunately you came to know that someone locked a queen turkey in a cage. No one is there to help that turkey. You have to help the Turkey to escape from this mysterious turkey forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
