Love Maker Escape is a type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. In this Valentine?s Day an evil cupid want to stop the love in this earth so he trapped a love cupid couple and take away from out of the world. He is locked the cupid boy in a heart cave and tied the cupid girl in another place. If that cupid couple will be not release from there then love will be end from the earth. You have to save the love in this earth so rescue the love cupid to escape from that evil pace. First of all you have to trap the evil and release the cupid girl. After release the girl you have to rescue the boy with help of girl. Search the clue and objects and solve some puzzle to rescue the love cupid and save the love in this earth. Good Luck. Have a fun.
