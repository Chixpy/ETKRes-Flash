Halloween Hotel Escape is a type of point and click new escape game developed by games2rule.com. You went out from your own to famous another city for celebrating the Halloween and you stayed in a hotel. Now finally Halloween night came and you are fully ready to go out for celebration. You came out from your hotel?s room but you see no one is in the hotel and by mistake the hotel staff locked you from outside and everyone went for celebrating Halloween. No one there for your help. Now what will you do? You came here for specially celebrating Halloween but how you will celebrate because you are trapped now. Can you escape from this Halloween Hotel? It is great challenge for you to escape from the hotel. You have to escape from here by solving the puzzles in each place. Click on objects to interact and solve puzzles. Good luck and have fun!Happy Halloween.
