Old Factory Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game you came to see a old factory for some inspection work. After spending few hours you decided to move. But you came to know that someone trapped you inside that old factory. You forget the way to exit. No one is there to help you out. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Try to find the key of the door. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
