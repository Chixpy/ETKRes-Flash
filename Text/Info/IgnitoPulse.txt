What would happen if you mixed the gameplay style of Megaman, Geometry Wars and Snake? – IgnitoPulse. Travel around neon arenas in two different game modes collecting as many glowing orbs as you can, all the while fighting to blast a relentless onslaught of enemies. Run, jump and shoot your way to victory on the high score boards while collecting powerups to help you along the way. How long can you survive for in the world of IgnitoPulse? This is a game caters to the little bit of hardcore gamer in every game fan – can you survive the intense challenge? More importantly – can you be the best?
