Treasure finder is point and click game developed by 8B Games. In this game series it contains 5 levels that has continuity. On Completing of each level you will get one special item, and you can use the same item for the next level. And it includes some hidden items and solving interesting puzzles etc. At last you will find the treasure at the end of this series. These games will be realeased in our website on weekly basis. Good luck and have fun!
