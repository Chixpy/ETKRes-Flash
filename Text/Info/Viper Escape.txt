Viper Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. This is the story of a viper. He has a castle in middle of a dangerous forest. Daily he will come out from his castle for search of some food. One day some mongoose saw the viper and they want to kill him. One day they waited for viper in his castle doors and trapped in their place. In this escape game you have to help the viper to escape back to his castle. But, first you have to move the any one of the mongoose from the door. So use your escaping mind and think about to escape the viper and get back to his castle soon. Good Luck Have Fun!
