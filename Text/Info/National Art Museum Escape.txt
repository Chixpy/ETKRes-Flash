National Art Museum Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to see the national art museum. After a long time when you decide to move out. But unfortunately, you noticed that the door of the museum is locked from outside. You have to find the way to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
