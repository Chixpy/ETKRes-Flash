Thanksgiving Adventurous Forest Escape is another new point and click live escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit an adventurous forest outside of your town for this Thanksgiving party. But unfortunately you missed the way out from the forest. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this forest by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
