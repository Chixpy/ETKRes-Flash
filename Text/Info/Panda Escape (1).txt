Panda Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Wowescape.com. In this brand new escape game one cute small panda unfortunately got trapped in the forest. He went faraway inside forest and forget the way to go back. How he will escape from there. Do you like to help this cute panda? Use your escaping mind for this cute panda, escape him from there and help him to go back home. Good Luck, Have fun!
