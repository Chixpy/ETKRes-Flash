Scary October Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you came to see the beauty of a forest which is located outer of your city. But unfortunately, you missed the way out. Being a Halloween time, the place looks very scary. You have to find the way to escape by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
