Mini Escape 11 is another type of point and click new escape game developed by wowescape.com. This is the 11th escape game of this mini escape series. We hope enjoying our every game of this series. After successfully escaped from previous mini escape games this is the new part of mini escape. In this game you are trapped inside a House. The door is locked by yourself from inside but unfortunately you loss the door key. So you have to escape from there by find the some clues, Objects which is inside that mini house and then solve some interesting puzzles for finding the door key. Use your best escaping skills to escape from that house. Good luck and have fun!
