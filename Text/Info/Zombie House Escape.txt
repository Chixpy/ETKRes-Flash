Zombie House Escape is another new point and click escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you came to visit a house but unfortunately it is a zombie's house . The house looks very scary. You don't know to escape from that place. No one is there to help you. You have to escape from this zombie house by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
