Escape Panda Cub is another new point and click room escape game from wowescape.com. In this game someone trapped the panda cub in the forest. No one is there to help the panda cub to come out of that. Now you have to help panda cub to come out from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
