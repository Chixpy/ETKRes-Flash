If you are wanting to play pool but cannot go out, Pool Practice is great for you! In this game the player is presented with a pool table in which ten randomly placed red balls are placed. You will deside the position of the white ball and from there you will try to hit the red balls in the pockets. Your score will highten as you get red balls in the pockets for consecutive cues. Use your mouse and control the firction of the white ball. Become the Pool master playing Pool Practice! This game is free.
