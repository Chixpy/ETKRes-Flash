Dingy Room Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com. You are trapped in a dingy room. This room is looking so scary and you are alone there, you have heard some scary noises also at that house. You want to escape from there soon but you don't know way of escape is where. Use your observation skills find the useful object in that dingy room and solve the mysteries puzzles of the room and escape from there. Have a fun!
