Thanksgiving Pumpkin Pie Escape is another new point and click live escape game from wowescape.com. In this game, you friends locked you inside a house and gone to Thanksgiving party. No one is there to help you. You to find the pumpkin pie for party gift and you have to rescue from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
