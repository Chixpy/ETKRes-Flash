Christmas Decorated Room Escape is another new point and click room escape game from games2rule.com. In this game, you decorated your room for Christmas with your friends. Later you came to know that your friends locked you inside your room. Its already late you need to get ready for the Christmas party. You have to escape from there by finding useful objects, hints and solving puzzle. Click on the objects to interact with them and solve simple puzzles. Good Luck Have Fun!
