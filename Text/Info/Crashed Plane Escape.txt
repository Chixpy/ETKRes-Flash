Crashed Plane Escape is another type of point and click new escape game developed by Games2rule.com. You are very interested to drive plane. One day you got an opportunity to drive a plane alone. While driving the plane you came to know there were some technical problems in the plane. So you tried you land it but it was landed toughly. When came out of the plane, no one is there in that place and you don?t know which place is this. Your mind is not working and you are totally confused and scare in this place. You want to escape from this place immediately before something miserably happened. But the only way is you have give signal to the office. Search for some clues, hints and object which will be help you for giving signal and quickly escape from there! Good luck and have fun!
